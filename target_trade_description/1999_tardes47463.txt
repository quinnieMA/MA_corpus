- The company was established in 1972 with the main purpose of manufacturing and selling tires. In 1999, the company merged with Kumho Construction, which engages in construction and transportation, and is engaged in construction business as a core business.- In 2003, the company sold the tire business to Kumho Tire. In 2006, it split the leisure business and the terminal business division. In 2011, it split the high-speed business division.- Specialized in the airport construction sector, participated in the construction of domestic and overseas airports including Incheon International Airport, Jeju International Airport, Yangyang International Airport, and Dubai Al Maktoum Airport.
