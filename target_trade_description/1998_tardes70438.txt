The Group's principal activity is that of a management holding company for an international trade and services group, comprising the following operating units: Metro Cash and Carry: providing wholesale and retail services; Real (incl. Extra) (Hyper markets and Food Stores): providing products for all daily needs; Media Markt and Saturn (Consumer Electronics and Home Improvement Centres).From 13 July, 2017 METRO (now Ceconomy) is only a supplier in consumer electronics and does not include wholesale and food retail. The company is the platform for companies, concepts and brands in the field of consumer electronics.
