The company is one of Canada's major consumer products and services companies with operations in Canada and the United States. Its businesses include the manufacture and distribution of tobacco products; financial services organization and real estate management and investment; fast food restaurants; drug stores and small space specialty retailers. On February 1,2000 British American Tobacco Plc acquired the 58% shareholding in Imasco Ltd that it did not already own. Since then, Imasco Ltd no longer exists.
