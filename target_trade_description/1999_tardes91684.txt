MCN Energy Group, Inc. is an integrated energy company primarily involved in natural gas production, gathering, processing, transmission, storage and distribution and energy marketing. MCN has two major business groups, namely Diversified Energy and Gas Distribution. Diversified Energy operates through MCN Energy Enterprises Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of MCN, and serves as a holding company for MCN's non-utility businesses. Gas Distribution consists principally of MichCon, a natural gas utility serving approximately 1.2 million customers in more than 530 communities throughout Michigan.
