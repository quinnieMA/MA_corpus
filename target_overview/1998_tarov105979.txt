Internet Security Systems Ltd. is a private company engaged in the provision of IT security services and Internet security products. The company is also involved in the provision of professional and technical computer-related services and advice. In addition, Internet Security Systems is active in planning and designing computer systems. The company, formerly known as ISS Group Limited, was incorporated in January 1990. It is headquartered in Windsor, United Kingdom.Internet Security Systems is a subsidiary of Integrated Systems & Services Group, a private company involved in the provision of computer programming, data processing and other computer related services in the United Kingdom.
