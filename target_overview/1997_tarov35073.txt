The company, with registered office located in Hamilton, Bermuda, is primarily engaged in the research, development, manufacture and distribution of Cardiac Thrombolytic Agents and DAT (Drugs of Abuse Testing) reagents in Bermuda and abroad, through its subsidiaries. The company is one of the leading suppliers of Cardiac Thrombolytic Agents in the United States. In the year of 1998, the company was acquired by the Switzerland-based Roche Group, which is one of the world's leading research-focused healthcare groups in the fields of pharmaceuticals and diagnostics.
