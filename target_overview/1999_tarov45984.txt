This company is engaged in the production and distribution of construction materials in Sweden. Its registered office is in Stockholm, Sweden. This company specializes in producing a wide range of cement, ready-mixed concrete, aggregates, concrete products, and alternative fuels. It is Europe's sixth-largest cement and building materials firm and is strongly positioned in mainly the Baltic Sea and North Sea regions. It is also a leading player in the international cement trading market, with operations in the U.S., West Africa, and Asia. This company is active in approximately 30 countries and has approximately 11,000 employees. It manages a subsidiary company, Cementa, the only cement producer in Sweden, with plants in Slite and Degerhamn, both on islands outside the southeast coast, and Skovde in the southern part of Sweden.
