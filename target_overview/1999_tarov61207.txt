This company is engaged in the provision of commercial property consultancy services. It was incorporated in 1988. It was formerly known as Lambert Smith Hampton (City) PLC. The registered head office of the company is located in London, United Kingdom.The company provides strategic consultancy services for corporate occupiers, property developers, and investors. It also offers a range of consultancy services in the areas of acquisitions, disposals, and lettings; capital markets; planning and development; building and lease; property management; business rates; legal support; and valuations. The company serves private and public sectors in retail, finance, banking, aviation, coach and bus, hospitality and leisure, motor trade and roadside, ports, rail, science parks, telecommunications, and transport industries.
