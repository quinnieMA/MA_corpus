It is an American company engaged in the retail of health and wellness products, including vitamins, minerals, herbal, and specialty supplements, sports nutrition products and diet products. The company was incorporated in 2007, and is headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States.The company operates through its main subsidiary GNC Corporation, which is formerly known as General Nutrition Centers, Inc. In 2007, GNC Corporation was sold by Apollo Advisors to Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan and Ares Management for approximately USD 1.6 billion.The company is the largest global specialty retailer of nutritional supplements. It sells its products through a worldwide network of more than 5,800 store locations operating under the GNC brand name. Its worldwide network includes 2,529 GNC-owned stores in the United States, 132 GNC-owned stores in Canada, 1,123 United States franchised stores, 873 international franchise stores in 48 other countries and 1,160 GNC store-within-a-store locations under its alliance with Rite Aid Corporation. Furthermore, the GNC-owned and franchised U.S. stores are located in shopping malls and strip centers.The company's GNC stores stock a wide range of weight loss/bodybuilding supplements and nutritional supplements, in both their own numerous in-house brands as well as third party name brands, in addition to health and fitness books, and magazines. These stores also stock vitamins, natural remedies, and health and beauty products. The company has substantial businesses in Australia. Its online partner is drugstore.com.
