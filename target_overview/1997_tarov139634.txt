The private limited company, formerly known as KJW Ventures Limited, is engaged in the sale of motor vehicle parts and accessories. It was incorporated in December of 1987 and has its registered head office located in Leeds, United Kingdom.The company is one of the UK's largest automotive accessory suppliers stocking over 18,500 automotive products in 19 branches throughout the country, with over 3200 products available for next day delivery. It offers a wide array of automotive replacement parts and accessories such as headlights, taillights, mirrors, bumpers, fenders, spark plugs, hoods, doors, panels, lights, lamps, lenses, radiators, shock absorbers, deflectors, reflectors, batteries, and spoilers. In addition, the company provides repair and maintenance, leasing, and financing services of vehicles.
