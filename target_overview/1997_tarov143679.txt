The company is a private limited firm primarily engaged in the operation of a chain of retail stores. The company, formerly known as Zap Stores Limited, was incorporated in April of 1990. The registered head office of the company is located in Leeds, United Kingdom.The company specializes in the retail distribution of a wide range of products, such as apparel, furniture, appliances and home furnishings, paint, hardware, toiletries, cosmetics, photographic equipment, jewelry, toys, clothing products and accessories, shoes and other footwear products, handbags, and sporting goods, among others. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Retail Holdings S.A.R.L., a holding company based in Luxembourg.
