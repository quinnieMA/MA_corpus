This company is engaged in the retail distribution of consumer electronics. The registered business office of the company is located in Lorenskog, Norway. It was incorporated in 1988. It operates as a subsidiary of United Kingdom-based Dixons Carphone plc. The company's products include notebooks, hardware, printer, keyboards and mice, software, network, fax machines, telephones, mobile phones, mobile accessories, digital cameras, photo frames, flash memory, camcorders, televisions, projectors, car stereos, washing machines, refrigerators, dishwashers, cookers, microwave ovens, ventilators, audio accessories, digital televisions and toys. The company is active and operational in Norway.
