Ajilon Group Limited, formerly known as Delphi Group Limited, operates as a private holding company for a group engaged in the provision of professional services in IT (Information Technology) to computer users. The company was incorporated in March of 1973 and has its registered head office in Borehamwood, United Kingdom. As a holding company, Ajilon takes controlling and supervisory measures and provides technical and scientific management holding services to its operating companies and enterprises, including the local units. Core services include management consulting and open-end management investments, business support services, and other investment advisorsfinancial services.The company's wholly-owned subsidiaries include Ajilon Payroll Services Limited, which is engaged in the provision of recruitment consultancy services supplying temporary staff; Ajilon (UK) Limited (formerly known as Computer People Limited), which specializes in specific IT sectors in both permanent and contract recruitment; and The Span Consultancy Limited, which is engaged in the provision of consultancy services to computer users.
