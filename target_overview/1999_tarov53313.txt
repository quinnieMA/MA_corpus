The company is primarily engaged in the operation of a chain of women's clothing stores. It is involved in the retail trade of clothing and accessories. It was incorporated in September of 1985. The company has a registered office located in London, United Kingdom.The company distributes and sells a wide range of clothing and accessories for women, including t-shirt, polo shirt, blouses, pants, shorts, skirts, jackets, hoodies, sweaters, legwear, underwear, dresses, beachwear, maternity clothes, sleepwear, socks, bags, rings, earrings, necklaces, bracelets, hair accessories, belts, watches, luggage, sunglasses, wallets, perfumes and colognes, hats and scarves. The company operates principally in the United Kingdom.
