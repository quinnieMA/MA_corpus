The company is a UK-based firm engaged in the development, manufacture and supply of extruded and baked pet foods. It is a privately held company incorporated in 1955. Its registered head office is strategically located in London, United Kingdom. The company is principally involved in producing a variety of dog and cat food from ingredients, such as grains, oilseed mill products, and meat products. These preparations may be canned, frozen, or dry, and are health-related or specialized type. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Nestle UK Ltd., which is the second largest company in the UK's yogurt and fromage frais sector.
