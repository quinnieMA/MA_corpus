The company, formerly known as HHCL United Limited, operates as an advertising agency and marketing communications firm in the United Kingdom. The company conducts business from its registered office located in London, the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in August 1987.The company primarily creates advertising campaigns and placing such advertising in periodicals, newspapers, radio and television, or other media. United London Communications provides a full range of advertising and marketing services including advice, creative services, account management, production of advertising material, media planning, and buying. It has more than 40 employees.
