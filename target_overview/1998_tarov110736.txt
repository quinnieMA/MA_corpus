This company is engaged in provision of commercial printing services. It was incorporated in 1983 and conducts business from its registered office located in Leicester, United Kingdom.The company's business operation is primarily involved in commercial printing without publishing. Printing processes included are letterpress printing and engraving printing. It is also engaged in commercial printing on purchased stock materials, such as stationery, invitations, labels, and similar items, on a job order basis. It offers a variety of services such as lithographic, gravure, screen, and flexographic printing on purchased stock materials; digital printing on graphical materials, and printing manifold business forms including checkbooks. These products and services are provided and distributed domestically.
