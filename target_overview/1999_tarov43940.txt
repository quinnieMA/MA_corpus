This UK-based company is engaged in the retail sale of motor vehicles, specializing in automobiles. It was incorporated in November 1997 and has its registered head office strategically located in Leicester, United Kingdom.The company primarily offers new and used cars. It sells cars from various brands, including Alpina, Audi, Bentley, BMW, Chrysler, Dodge, Ferrari, Honda, Jaguar, Jeep, Land Rover, Lamborghini, Lexus, Maserati, Mercedes-Benz, and Porsche, among others. The company principally conducts its business operations within the United Kingdom. It is affiliated with Barclays Bank Plc, which serves as its business banker.
