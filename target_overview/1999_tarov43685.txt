The company is an entity based in the United Kingdom that is engaged in the provision of computer programming services. It was incorporated in May of 1990. The registered business office of the company is located in Newcastle Upon Tyne, UK.The company is principally involved in providing services that include content management, filtering and conversion software products; electronic content management consulting services; and postcontract software maintenance and support. It also offers systems software development service, web platform development software, compiler and decompiler software, configuration management software, development environment software, enterprise application integration software, and graphical user interface development software. The company serves both private and public organizations.
