The company is a private entity primarily engaged in manufacturing, knitting, finishing and marketing women's, misses', and girls' full-length and knee-length sheer hosiery. Its products are marketed through a network of dealers and sales agents in the United Kingdom. In addition, the company employs highly skilled professional staff capable of catering various customers, including corporations, business professionals, small and large groups, and other business individuals. Formerly know as Highcross Hosiery (Leicester) Limited, the company was incorporated in 1971 with it head office in Nottingham, UK. Moreover, its business operations are handled and administered by Brian Stuart Davis and Keith Harrison.
