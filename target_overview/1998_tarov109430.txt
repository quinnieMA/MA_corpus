This company, with registered office located in London, United Kingdom, is a property investment and development company that carries out house building activities throughout the United Kingdom. The company was incorporated in July 1936 and was formerly known as Percy Bilton plc. The company owns and manages a portfolio of over 100 residential estates comprising the former residential land developed by Percy Bilton Homes, once a significant nationwide house-builder. It acquired the portfolio from SEGRO Plc, Europe's leading owner-manager and developer of industrial property. The company is a wholly-owned subsidiary of SEGRO plc (formerly Slough Estates plc), a property investment and development company focused on the provision of flexible business space in Europe and the United States. SEGRO is headquartered in the United Kingdom and has a listing on the London Stock Exchange and on the Euronext in Paris.
