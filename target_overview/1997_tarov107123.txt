This company is engaged in the manufacture and sale of coatings and printing inks. It was founded in 1773 and has its registered business office based in Wolverhampton, United Kingdom. It was formerly known as Manders Limited. The company offers offset/newspaper inks, water-based liquid packaging inks, and heatset Web offset inks. It has state-of-the-art technology and equipment for manufacturing, as well as employs certified staff skilled in manufacturing operations in compliance with the standards and other technical specifications. It operates as a subsidiary of Flint Group S.A., which is headquartered in Luxembourg.
