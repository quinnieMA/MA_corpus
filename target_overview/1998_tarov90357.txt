This company, formerly known as Kemira Polymers Ltd., is engaged in the manufacture of polyurethane systems and elastomers. It was incorporated in 1994. The registered business office of the company is based in High Peak, United Kingdom. The company offers microcellular elastomers for impact, shock, and vibration management in suspension and chassis applications; polyurethane elastomer for rotational coatings; polyurethane systems for a range of customized solutions, including mold making and patterns, and furniture edgings; microcellular polyurethane elastomers for use in industrial vibration isolation applications; sprayable polyurethane systems for concrete maintenance applications; single component polyurethane elastomers for rollers, seals, and gaskets; and polyurethane encapsulation products for light rail. It also provides waterproof roofing membrane, polyurethane rail ballast stabilization, and waterproofing and anti-slip surfacing systems. It serves automotive, civil engineering, manufacturing, marine, offshore, rail, transport, and electrical and electronic industries in the United Kingdom and internationally. The company operates as a subsidiary of Dow Chemical Company Limited.
