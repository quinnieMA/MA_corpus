The company, with headquarters located in Dallas, USA, is the parent company of one of the world's leading general building and construction management firms, Turner Construction. It was established in 1902 by Henry C. Turner. The corporation is a subsidiary of the world leading construction services provider HOCHTIEF AG. Based in Essen, Germany HOCHTIEF designs, finances, builds, and operates complex projects of all kinds. Its subsidiary, Turner Construction, is also one of the largest commercial builders in the United States. This company provides services for large, medium-sized and smaller projects as well as interior construction and renovation services. Some of its large projects are Madison Square Garden, the UN Secretariat, and the Taipei 101 tower.The company, through its subsidiary Turner International LLC, is active in Africa, Asia-Pacific, Europe, Latin America and the Middle East. Turner International provides comprehensive management services through the development, design and construction phases of general building projects.
