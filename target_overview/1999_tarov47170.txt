The company is considered as a privately-held entity which is engaged in the manufacture of a wide array of fabricated metal products. It was incorporated in the year 1958 and conducts its business from its corporate head office located in Azpeita, Spain. It is primarily engaged in manufacturing fabricated metal products (forgings and stampings, cutlery and hand tools, architectural and structural metals, boilers, tanks, shipping containers, hardware, spring and wire products, machine shop products, turned products, screws, and nuts and bolts) offered to a variety of customers and industries in the region.
