This company, based in the United Kingdom, is recognized a leader in the development, registration and marketing of pharmaceutical products for global animal health markets. It was founded by Peter Lawrence and Barry Day in 1972; and conducts business from its registered head office located in New Malden. The company's proprietary patented drug, Aivlosin is a macrolide antibiotic for treatment of respiratory and enteric diseases in pigs and poultry. Its generic drugs - Ecomectin, Ecoheart, Ecotraz and Ecomintic (endectocides and other antiparasitics) - are used for treatment and prevention of parasites in cattle, sheep, pigs, horses and dogs; and Chlortet and Oxytet - used for treatment of bacterial infections in pigs, poultry and cattle.
