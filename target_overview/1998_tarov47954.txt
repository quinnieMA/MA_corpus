The company is a holding firm whose subsidiaries are engaged in the manufacture, supply and shipping of clinker and cement; chartering of vessels; and management and operation of a jetty. It was incorporated in 1958 and has its registered business address located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.The company handles the administrative affairs and services of a holding company and grants management services to its subsidiaries. It also provides financial support and control function for the board. It is responsible for managing the group and its overall legal structure, tax planning, financial and equity structures. In addition, the company is involved in matters of policy, strategic planning, marketing, selecting and manning senior management positions, approving investments and budgets, and the overall ongoing monitoring of the group companies' performance.
