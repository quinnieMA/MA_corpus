This company is engaged in providing energy and utility services. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Florianopolis, Brazil. The company was founded in 1997. The company specialises in the generation, commercialisation, and transmission of electricity, as well as the transportation of natural gas. The company is at the forefront of renewable energy, providing solutions that include solar and wind power. The company focusses on enhancing energy efficiency, optimising energy use, and supporting decarbonisation efforts. The company delivers tailored energy solutions and performance guarantees, helping businesses and municipalities achieve their sustainability goals while managing their energy resources effectively.
