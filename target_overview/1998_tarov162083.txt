This company is engaged in the provision of product development consultancy, health economics and outcomes research, real-world disease understanding, marketing research, medical communications, and post-meeting brand support services. It offers services in areas such as pricing and reimbursement for market access, strategic marketing consultants, international market research, local/multi-local market research, creative and events, marketing support services and market research, communications and promotion, medical communications and health education, and real-world research and disease programs. The company also provides services in areas that include marketing support services, marketing communications, health value, economics, and outcomes for market access, communications and brand planning, economics and pricing, and public policy. Formerly known as Adelphi Communications Limited, it was incorporated in 1986. The registered business office of the company is located in London, United Kingdom.
