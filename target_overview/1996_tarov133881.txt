Clyde Petroleum Limited is an active private company whose group is engaged in the exploration and acquisition of oil and gas properties and their development and production on an international basis. The company, formerly known as Clyde Petroleum (1987) Public Limited Company which lasted in July 1987, was incorporated in February of 1987. The registered head office of the company is primarily based in the city of Aberdeen in Grampian, United Kingdom.Clyde Petroleum comprises the activities in the extraction of crude petroleum and natural gas, oil and gas extraction and other service activities incidental to oil and gas extraction excluding surveying. The company is also engaged in the management activities of holding companies including head offices.The company is one of the wholly owned subsidiaries of ConocoPhillips, an active publicly quoted company engaged as an international, integrated energy company.
