The firm is a private industrial company engaged in the provision of refuse systems and sanitary services, as well as the collection and treatment of waste and scrap principally in Germany. The registered head office of the company is located in Duisburg, Germany.The firm comprises establishments primarily involved in collecting and hauling hazardous waste, nonhazardous waste, and recyclable materials within a local area, as well as operating hazardous and nonhazardous waste transfer stations. The company is responsible for the identification, treatment, packaging, and labeling of waste for the purposes of transport. It operates under the supervision and management of Chairman Clemens J. Vedder.
