The company, with registered head office located in Edinburgh, United Kingdom, is engaged in the provision of innovative outsourcing solutions in the field of facilities management and infrastructure services. It delivers high quality infrastructure and services in education, health and local government, on the roads and across the rail network. The company is also a market provider in track renewals and rail maintenance. It is also involved in highways maintenance, manufacturing and installing specialist products, surfaces and signage. The company also delivers a full range of solutions to help public service and other providers build, maintain and operate their facilities. These public service and other providers range from schools, colleges and student rooms, to offices, health centres and local government buildings. The company was incorporated in 1995.
