The company, formerly known as Blakes Haulage Limited, is engaged in the provision of haulage services. It was incorporated in the year 1975 and has its registered head office located in Leeds, United Kingdom. The company offers an array of services, such as completing inbound carrier's receiving documents; unloading and delivery of the goods to the booth/stand space from the receiving dock; storing of empty cartons/crates and extra products at on-site warehouse; picking up of goods from the booth/stand space to the receiving dock and loading back into the carrier; and completing outbound carrier's shipping documents. It principally conducts its business operations within the country.
