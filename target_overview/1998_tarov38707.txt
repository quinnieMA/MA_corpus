This Firm operates as a holding company for subsidiaries involved in the production and distribution of electricity, water and gas throughout Germany. Formerly known as Deutsche Kontinentale Gas-Gesellschaft, it has its registered head office in Munchen, Germany. The company's subsidiary, E.ON Avacon AG, provides a comprehensive range of electricity, gas, heat, and water services to approximately 1.4 million customers. Its service territory spans just on 28,100 square kilometers in central and eastern Lower Saxony and in the Northern and Central Saxony-Anhalt. More than 40 municipal and community utilities sourced their energy from Avacon. Another subsidiary of the company, Thuega AG, is engaged in the generation and distribution of electricity and gas, as well as the distribution of water. It also offers advice on legal, commercial and technical problems with reference to the supply of energy and water. The company was incorporated in 1855.
