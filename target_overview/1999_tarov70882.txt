This company operates as a holding firm for a group engaged in the manufacture of aluminum products. It also has active holding and controlling interests in companies active in the fields of pharmaceutical and cosmetics packaging, food and tobacco packaging, fine chemicals and specialties, intermediates and additives, and primary materials. It was incorporated in 1889 and has its registered office located in Zurich, Switzerland.The company's group operates through two divisions: Alusuisse Primary Materials, and Alusuisse Fabricated Products Lawson. Its activities in the production of aluminium are primary smelting and refining of nonferrous metals. The company is a recognised partner of leading multinational companies. It has active operations in Europe, America, Australia, and the Far East. Its leading competitor is Chirex Incorporated. Icelandic Aluminium Co. Ltd. (ISAL), which operates a smelter in Straumsvik, Iceland, is part of the international operations of Alusuisse Lonza Holdings.The company is an international diversified group that is registered in the Euronext (European Stock Exchange), Europe's leading cross-border exchange originally created in 2000 from the merger of the Amsterdam, Brussels and Paris stock exchanges.
