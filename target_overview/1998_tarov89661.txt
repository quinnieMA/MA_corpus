Sheffield Forgemasters Group Limited is a UK-based private company that operates as an intermediate holding company of a group of companies engaged in the manufacture of steel forgings. The company, formerly known as Impactmargin Public Limited, was incorporated in November of 1995 and adopted the name Sheffield Forgemasters Group Limited in May of 1996. The company has its registered head office located in Leeds, United Kingdom.As a holding company, Sheffield Forgemasters Group is responsible for the development of common strategies and standards for all businesses in the group, supervision of their implementation, and monitoring of the use of assets. Moreover, it assumes the functions of developing innovation, investment, and personnel policy, and provides security for the business activities of the group companies.Sheffield Forgemasters Group Limited, which conducts the majority of its business activities in the UK, is globally owned by Atchison Casting Corp, a US-based publicly quoted company, traded on the OTC Market, USA under ticker symbol AHNCQ.
