This company is engaged in the generation, transmission and distribution of electricity in Chile. It was incorporated in December 1943 and has its head office located in Santiago. The company, together with its subsidiaries and affiliates, owns and operates approximately 179 hydroelectric, thermal and wind power generating units, which have a total installed capacity of more than 13,000 megawatts (MW). In addition, it is involved in the provision of a broad range of engineering and construction services, as well as consultancy services. The company is a subsidiary of the Italy-based Enel S.p.A., which is one of the largest energy companies in the world. It provides electricity distribution to over 2 million customers via Enel Distribution Chile, electricity generation through 108 units by Enel Generation Chile, renewable energy from hydroelectric, wind, photovoltaic, and geothermal sources, and energy solutions through Enel X Chile, including self-sufficient condominiums powered by photovoltaic energy and electromobility infrastructure in partnership with Hyundai.
