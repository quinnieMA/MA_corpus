This company operates as a holding firm whose subsidiaries are in the energy sector in the United States and abroad. It was formerly known as MCN Corp., which existed until July 1992. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Detroit, Michigan. The company was founded in 1988. This company operates as a diversified energy firm with markets and investments primarily in the Midwest-to-Northeast corridor. It operated through two major business groups: diversified energy and gas distribution. MCNEE was comprised of pipelines and processing; fuel management services and supplies gas to power generation facilities under long-term sales contracts; electric power, which provides fuel management services and supplies gas to power generation facilities under long term sales contracts; energy marketing, including the sale of premium, reliable bundled energy services to large volume customers; and natural gas and oil exploration, development, and production in Michigan.
