The company is a privately held firm active in the construction industry. The company primarily operates as a special trade contractor. The company started its business operation in September of 1988 and conducts business from its registered corporate office which is strategically located in Leeds, West Yorkshire, United Kingdom. It was formerly known as Quayshelfco 248 Limited.The company's principal activities include excavating and grading, demolition of buildings and other structures, septic system installation, and house moving. The company is also involved in the provision of earth moving and land clearing services. It mainly operates its business within the domestic market.
