The company is a leading manufacturer of metal roof and wall systems, including standing seam roofing systems. The company was incorporated in Texas in March of 1876. The registered head office of the company is located in Houston, Texas. The company has 16 sales and manufacturing facilities selling and delivering a wide range of metal products direct to many facets of the contracting community. Throughout the nation, the company manufactures preformed metal roofing, siding and light gauge framing for the commercial, industrial, architectural, residential and agricultural markets. It manufactures 90 percent of what it sells and provides support for both the conventional and pre-engineered building construction industries.The company aims to be the undisputed leader in the metal component industry by providing superior products and professional services. It strives to produce products of enduring quality that enhance the aesthetics, form, function and performance of structures in which people work, live, play, learn, worship, and use for storage and protection. The company has established strategic alliances with American Institute of Steel Construction, Inc.; Department of Community Affairs: Florida Building Code Information System; Galvalume Sheet Producers of North America; Metal Building Contractors and Erectors Association; Metal Construction Association; Miame-Dade County; National Roofing Contractors Association; North American Insulation Manufacturers Association; Underwriters Laboratories Inc.; Construction Specification Institute; ENERGY STAR Partnership; International Code Council; Metal Building Manufacturers Association; Metal Roofing Alliance; National Coil Coating Association; NCI Group, Inc.; and Self Storage Association. The company's plants and sales offices are located in Houston, Texas; Adel, Georgia; Atlanta, Georgia; Atwater, California; Ennis, Texas; Indianapolis, Indiana; Jackson, Mississippi; Lubbock, Texas; Memphis, Tennessee; Oklahoma City, Oklahoma; Omaha, Nebraska; Phoenix, Arizona; Richmond, Virginia; Rome, New York; Salt Lake City, Utah; and San Antonio, Texas.The company is part of NCI Building Systems, Inc., the largest integrated manufacturer and marketer of metal building components, custom engineered metal building systems and metal coil coaters in North America.
