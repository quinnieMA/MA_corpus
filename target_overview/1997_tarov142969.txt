This company is engaged in the operation of a professional football club and related activities such as leisure, entertainment and hotels. The company was previously known as Soccer Investments PLC. Its registered head office is located in Leicester, United Kingdom.The UK-based company operates as the parent company of Filbert Realisations No 1PLC, a company involved in the operation of professional sports clubs; Filbert Realisations No 2 Limited, a real estate firm primarily operating in the United Kingdom; and LCFC.COM Limited, a company that is engaged in computer systems and design and related services.As of June 2012 the company went out of business.
