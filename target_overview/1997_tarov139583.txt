The company, formerly known as Kitglen Limited, is engaged in the retail sale of upholstered leather furniture primarily in the United Kingdom. It is a public limited company which was incorporated in the year 1973. The registered head office of the company is located in London, United Kingdom. The company sells a wide range of upholstered leather furniture through its network of more than 30 stores within the United Kingdom. It has served various customers across the country. In addition, the company operates as one of the wholly owned subsidiaries of Uno Plc, which is engaged in the retail sale of upholstered furniture.
