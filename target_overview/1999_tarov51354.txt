This company is engaged in mining activities. It explores gold and other precious metal ores in Ghana. The company is also involved in the provision of mapping services and geophysical surveying. Anglogold Ashanti Ghana was incorporated in 2004. It operates from a registered business headquarters located in Accra, Ghana. One of the company's operating subsidiaries is Cluff Resources Limited, an industrial firm active in mining and quarrying in the United Kingdom. Another affiliate is Societe Ashanti Goldfields de Guinee S.A., a producer of gold in Guinea. The company's gold products are used primarily for fabrication purposes. These are applicable for electronics, dentistry, decorations, medals, and official coins. Gold is also a form of investment since it has a high value. It offers gold mining operations, silver production, exploration activities, mineral resource management, community investment, environmental management, and rigorous corporate governance standards. It aims to responsibly harness its global portfolio of gold mining operations and diverse mineral resources, while prioritizing environmental stewardship, community engagement, and adherence to rigorous governance standards.
