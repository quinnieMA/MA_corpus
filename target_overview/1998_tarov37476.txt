This company, headquartered in Kassel, Germany, is an independent, customer-focused supplier of mineral products for the agriculture, industry, consumer, and community sectors. It was established in 1889.The company offers potassium chloride for crops, such as grain, corn, rice, and soybean; fertilizer specialties that are used for crops with magnesium and sulfur requirements, including rapeseed and potatoes, as well as for chloride-sensitive crops consisting of citrus, grapes, and vegetables; and water-soluble fertilizers for use in fertigation of fruit and vegetables. It also provides melting salts, sodium chloride, natural rock salt, high-purity vaccum salt and natural sea salt, potassium chloride, Epsom salt, minerals for pets and livestock, aluminum recycling, salt licks for farm and wild animals. In addition, it offers table salts; salts for water treatment; dishwashing salts; and de-icing salts.
