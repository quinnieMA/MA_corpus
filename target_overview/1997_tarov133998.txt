This company is engaged in the provision of business and management holding services whose subsidiaries are involved the pharmaceutical industry. It was incorporated in 1977. The company operates from its registered business head office located in Sutton, United Kingdom. The company offers a wide range of prescription and online doctor consultation, medicines and treatments, health and wellness drugs, electrical health supplies, mobility and daily living products, mother and baby medicines and supplements, skincare items and toiletries, beauty and fragrance supplies, and fragrance clearance items. It also has a portfolio of products for allergies, suncare, slimming, and summer fragrance.
