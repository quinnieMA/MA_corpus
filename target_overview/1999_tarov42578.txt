This company is an entity based in Italy that is engaged in the publication of books and other related articles. The registered business office of the company is located in Milano, Italy. The company serves as one of the largest and important Italian media groups. It is principally involved in publishing books, specializing in children's books. It also offers magazines, newspapers, catalogs, technical books, and journals. In addition, it provides services such as printing, textbook and research publishing, author funded publishing services, engraving, bookbinding, reproduction services, photocopying and other related services. Further, it provides bindery and graphic design services. The company serves both public and private organizations. It has its active operations within the country.
