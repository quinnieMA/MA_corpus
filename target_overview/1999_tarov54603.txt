This company, with headquarters in Oslo, Norway, is one of the leading companies for construction-related services and project development in Norway. The company offers a complete range of services in the engineering and construction process of work premises, homes and infrastructure facilities. It was established in 1906. The company contributes its skills at all stages from ideas and development to construction, as well as operation and maintenance. It is also active throughout the value circle everything from small building service contracts to assuming total responsibility for identifying and solving the customers' long-term needs of construction-related services. Its operations are aimed at satisfying people's need for housing, work environments and communications opportunities. In addition, the company provides other mediation and management services to its projects. The company's mission is to develop, build and maintain the physical environment for living, traveling and working. The company is an active firm that primarily operates in Norway and is a subsidiary of Skanska Kraft AB, based in Sweden, a company engaged in a construction, real estate, general construction of buildings and civil engineering works.
