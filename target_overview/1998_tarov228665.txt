This company is engaged in the provision of management and investment holding services for a group active in the mechanical and electronic industry. It was incorporated in January 1994 and was formerly known as Vero Group PLC. As a holding company, it provides group planning and strategies as well as exercises financial control over its subsidiaries. It also settles the group's general policies and provides central services. It has a registered business office located in London in the United Kingdom. \Through its subsidiaries and affiliate firms, APW Electronics Group serves the electronics and telecommunications industries in the United Kingdom. It operates as a part of Applied Power Ltd., a British industrial company.
