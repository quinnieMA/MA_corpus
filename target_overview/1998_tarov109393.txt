The company is engaged in the sale, product development, customer-specific design and engineering, marketing, manufacturing and distribution of integrated pneumatic and fluid control systems for industrial and automotive applications. It was established in 1966 and has its registered business office located in Altavista, Virginia.The company specializes in valves, fluid control systems and component manufacturing and assembly. It also involves in aftermarket TPMS, air accessory and tire hardware distribution. These products ate shipped directly to OEM and aftermarket customers. It has advanced technology and equipment for manufacturing, as well as employs certified staff skilled in manufacturing operations in compliance with the standards other technical specifications. In addition, the company operates as a subsidiary of August Lux Holding, Company, which is based in Chicago, Illinois.
