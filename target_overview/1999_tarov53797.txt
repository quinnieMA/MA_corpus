This company delivers leading Internet media and market research solutions. It was incorporated in 1997 and has its registered head office located in New York, New York, USA. The company is identified as the global standard for Internet audience and measurement and ranks as the world's leading source for online advertising intelligence. Its service portfolio include panel-based and site-centric Internet audience measurement services, online advertising intelligence, user lifestyle and demographic data, and e-commerce & transaction metrics, as well as custom data, research and analysis. The following are some of the company's proprietary research tools and services: NetView, which provides a full-range view of activity on the web and in the digital media universe; AdRelevance, which is the leading online advertising intelligence-gathering tool that capture advertising activity across all major industries, channels, formats and platforms on the web; VideoCensus, which delivers end-to-end accounting of audience size, demographic composition, engagement, and competitive activity; and MegaPanel, which delivers detailed information about in-depth consumer attitudes and behavior by tracking more than a million people worldwide. The company operates in various countries, particularly in Austria, France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, South Africa, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, Australia, Brazil, China, Hong Kong, Japan, Singapore, and New Zealand. The company operates as a subsidiary of Nielsen Holdings N.V., a global information and measurement company.
