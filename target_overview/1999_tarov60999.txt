This company is engaged in providing customer experience solutions. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Daly City, California, United States of America. The company was founded in 1990 by a joint venture of multiple partners. The company specializes in contact center software, digital channels, AI and automation, workforce engagement, and customer journey management. It also operates branches in over 100 countries. Its products are sold and distributed in domestic market as well as overseas including more than 100 countries.The company is one of the leading providers of customer experience solutions in the domestic market and overseas. Its mission is to create inclusive and equitable workplaces and make a positive impact on the communities they serve. The company establishes partnerships with channel partners, strategic alliances, independent consultants, and technology partners. It serves customers including over 7,500 organizations.
