This company, formerly known as Companhia Paulista de Servico de Gas, is engaged in the wholesale distribution of natural gas. It was founded and incorporated in 1872. The registered business office of the company is located in Sao Paulo, Brazil. The company's activities are divided into six business segments: residential, which supplies piped natural gas to households; commercial, which maintains natural gas networks in commercial venues; industrial, which provides gas products to industrial plants; generation, which owns electricity-generating plants powered by natural gas; cogeneration, which produces thermal and electric power from gas combustion; automotive, which offers natural gas for vehicles through gasoline stations; and construction, which includes the development of infrastructure for gas distribution. It is considered the country's largest gas distributor, with around 1.6 million residential, commercial, and industrial customers, as of 2016, who receive gas through about 14.000 kilometers (8.699 mi) of pipelines.
