This company, through its subsidiaries, is involved in the manufacture and selling of a wide range of asbestos-containing products in England and other parts of the world, including the United States and Canada. In addition, the company, through a subsidiary, brokers raw asbestos fiber to companies in various parts of the world. The company serves the transportation, marine, petrochemical and power generation markets. The company has also established a joint-venture company to make powder metal transmission and engine components in Rajasthan, India. Its interests include gaskets, camshafts and powdered metals. The company was established in England in 1920 and has its registered office and corporate headquarters situated in Manchester, United Kingdom. It is owned by Federal-Mogul Corporation, a premier supplier of products, services and solutions to original equipment manufacturers.
