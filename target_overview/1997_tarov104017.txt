The firm, formerly known as Buteline Limited, is a privately held company which is engaged in the manufacture and distribution of automotive components. It was incorporated in November of 1992. The registered head office of the company is strategically located in Birmingham, United Kingdom. As part of its business activity, the company is also involved in the installation of automotive accessories. Its business operation is principally active in the United Kingdom. The company is affiliated with Pricewaterhousecoopers Llp, the largest professional services firm in the world.
