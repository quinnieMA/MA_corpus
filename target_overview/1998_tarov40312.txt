J&L Specialty Steel, LLC is privately held company engaged in the production of flat-rolled stainless steel. The company was incorporated in 1990. The registered head office of the company is located in Coraopolis, Pennsylvania.J&L Specialty Steel is one of North America's largest stainless steel producers with an estimated annual melting capacity of approximately 600,000 tons. The company produces steel products used for various applications such as home decor, lighting, furniture, kitchen and gardening, among others.In February of 2004, the company was acquired by Allegheny Technologies, Inc. (one of the largest and most diversified specialty metals producers in the world).
