The firm is a private industrial company engaged in the design, manufacture and sale of a range of communications equipment, including telephone and telegraph apparatus, and television and radio transmitters, principally in France. The registered head office of the company is located in Paris, France.The firm comprises its business activities in manufacturing wire telephone and data communications equipment. These products may be standalone or board-level components of a larger system. Examples of products made by the company are central office switching equipment, cordless telephones (except cellular), PBX equipment, telephones, telephone answering machines, LAN modems, multi-user modems, and other data communications equipment, such as bridges, routers, and gateways.
