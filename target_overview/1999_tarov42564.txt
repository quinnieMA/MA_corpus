Gorsey Six Limited, formerly known as Eclipse Blinds Limited, is a private limited company that operates as an investment and financing company for a group engaged in the distribution of windows systems and component parts in the United Kingdom and overseas. The company was incorporated in October of 1946. The registered head office of the company is located in Birmingham, United Kingdom. Gorsey Six provides group planning and strategy and in particular exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top managements, and provides central services. As of November 2007, the company operates as a part of Headlam Group Plc.
