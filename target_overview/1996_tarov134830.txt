The company is a privately held firm which operates as a nursing home for senior individuals. It was incorporated in 1987 and has a registered head office located in Glasgow G2 2QZ, United Kingdom.The privately held company primarily provides skilled nursing services in the home, along with a range of the following services: personal care services, homemaker and companion services, physical therapy, medical social services, medications, medical equipment and supplies, counseling, 24-hour home care, and occupation and vocational therapy. In addition, the company provides other services, such as dietary and nutritional services, speech therapy, audiology, and high-tech care such as intravenous therapy.
