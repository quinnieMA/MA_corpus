EX BUP Limited is an investment holding company whose subsidiaries are engaged in the publishing, printing, and distribution of newspapers. The company was incorporated on March 1932. The company was formerly known as Bristol United Press Limited.EX BUP provides group planning and strategy and in particular exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top managements, and provides central services.EX BUP manages the following subsidiaries: South Western Broadcasting Limited, Morley Adams Limited, Avon cable New Limited, Mercury Publications Limited, and Southern and Western Press Limited.EX BUP's global ultimate owner is Luce Holdings Limited. The company is also a wholly owned subsidiary of EX HM (formerly known as Harmsworth Media), primarily engaged in the provision of financial intermediation.EX BUP is a private limited company with share capital. It is a small unquoted company filing only a modified balance sheet. The company is based in the United Kingdom with registered office located in London.
