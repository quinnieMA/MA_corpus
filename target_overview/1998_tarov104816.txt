It is a private limited company primarily engaged in the manufacture of industrial valves. The company manufactures industrial valves and valves for water works and municipal water systems. Incorporated in 1962, the US-based company has a registered office located in Farmington, Connecticut.The company is a subsidiary of Norgren Limited, one of the three largest global suppliers of pneumatic motion and fluid control solutions. Norgren is at the forefront of pneumatic motion and fluid control technology, whether this is through the supply of standard pneumatic components or engineering advantage solutions in sectors.
