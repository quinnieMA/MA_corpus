The company, with registered office address in Perth, Washington, is engaged in mining activities. It was incorporated in the year 1925. The firm specializes in the exploration, mining and management of iron ores in Western Australia. It operates a 100% interest in the Koolyanobbing iron ore project and a 50% interest in the Cockatoo Island iron ore project, located off the northwest of Western Australia. The Koolyanobbing iron ore project is compost of three mine sites, Koolyanobbing, Mt Jackson and Windarling as well as its ore processing facilities are located. In 2006, the company supplied ore products of more than 6,704 thousand tons. The company supplies iron ore to the Chinese and Japanese markets. It aims to improve returns from its existing businesses, deliver long term growth in earnings per share and seek growth opportunities to provide enhanced shareholder return through exploration success, incremental expansion opportunities and prudent acquisitions.
