The company is a private firm engaged in the provision of waste management and related services in the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in the year 1991 and conducts its business from its registered head office located in London W6 8RX. The company provides a wide range of solid and hazardous waste management services, including collection, transportation, storage, treatment, recycling and the disposal of waste. It also operates a waste-to-energy facility, develops and operates water and wastewater treatment facilities and performs certain related services. The company provides services to more than 18 countries worldwide. The company operates as a subsidiary of Waste Management Inc., which is a leading provider of comprehensive trash and waste removal, recycling, and environmentally safe waste management services.
