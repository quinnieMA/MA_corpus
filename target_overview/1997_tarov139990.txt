The company operates as a senior Canadian oil and gas exploration, production and marketing firm. Its registered head office is strategically located in Regina Saskatchewan S4p 3c4, Canada. The company's business operation specializes in the exploration and development of crude oil and natural gas in northwestern Canada, as well as focuses on a full range of crude and natural gas marketing activities, transportation and with interest in cogeneration. The firm aims in expanding its operations geographically to other provinces and abroad to South America, Africa, Asia, and Europe. It was acquired by Canadian Occidental Petroleum Ltd, which is also engaged in oil and gas exploration and production activities.
