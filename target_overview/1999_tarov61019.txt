Channel Holdings Limited is a UK-based holding company whose principal subsidiaries are engaged in the supply of physical car security products to the major motor manufactures in the UK and to motor accessory retailers in the UK and overseas; manufacture and distribute a wide range of products for the security alarm industry. The company, formerly known as Channel Holdings PLC, was incorporated in the year 1881 and adopted the name Channel Holdings Limited in June of 2000. Its registered head office is located in Gosport, United Kingdom. As a holding company, Channel Holdings is involved in the administration and management services such as the provision of group planning and strategy. The company is also involved in the settlement of general policy as well as appointment of top managements for its holding companies.
