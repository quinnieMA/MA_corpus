This company is engaged in the wholesale of vitro diagnostics, laboratory products, medical equipment and consumables, physiotherapy and fitness equipment, as well as the provision of related services. The company was incorporated in 1988. Its registered business office is based in Oslo, Norway. The company's mission is to give improved solutions for analysis, diagnostics, and treatment, and contribute to added value and satisfaction for its customers. Its vision is to be the first choice within its market segment and to be attractive to competent employees.
