This is a marine transportation service company primarily engaged in the transportation of petrochemical gases and liquefied petroleum gas (LPG), ship-to-ship transfer of crude oil and LNG, and the design and construction of smaller and specialized high quality marine vessels. It is a fully integrated shipping company that designs, builds, owns, mans and manages its own ships. The foundation stone of the company was laid in 1916, when Isak Martinius Skaugen, Captain of the four-masted barque 'Alcides', established his company D/S-A/S Eikland. The company's corporate headquarters is located in Oslo, Norway.It is structured into three activities: gas activities performed by Norgas Carriers AS that is involved in gas transportation, recovery and purging; China activities located in Shanghai, concentrated on LPG river transportation and a training center to train seafarers in handling dangerous cargoes; and Shutter tanker activities performed by Skaugen Petro Trans, Inc., providing crude oil transfer, primarily in the Gulf of Mexico. The company's principal customers are companies active within the oil and gas industry.In 2003, it joined forces with Teekay Shipping Corporation to jointly operate and expand its business allowing Teekay to take a 50 percent stake. The company also forged an strategic alliance with Taizhou Wuzhou Shipbuilding Co Ltd and Shenghui Gas & Chemical Systems, both Chinese companies, which specializes in structured cryogenic steel tanks used in the marine and petrochemical industry.Its common stock is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange where it trades under the symbol IMSK. The company is operational through 13 subsidiaries worldwide, with activities in Oslo, Norway; Freeport and Houston, the United States; Nanjing, Wuhan and Shanghai, China; St. Petersburg, Russia; Singapore and Dubai, the United Arab Emirates.
