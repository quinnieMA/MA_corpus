This company operates as a holding company whose subsidiaries are engaged in fire security, marine instrumentation and liquid measurement and control. It was incorporated in 1920. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Stockton-on-tees, United Kingdom. The company is involved in holding the securities of its subsidiaries for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms. In addition, it is also responsible in the provision of a range of corporate services for its subsidiaries including the areas of financing, investment, legal affairs, tax affairs and other related services.
