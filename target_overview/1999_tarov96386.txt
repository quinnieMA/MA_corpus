This company is engaged in the provision of real estate services. It was incorporated in 1989. The registered business office of the company is located in Oslo, Norway. The company covers project development and management, financing, risk management, as well as the sale and rental of residential, commercial and retail projects to owners, tenants and investors. It also caters other activities related to real estate. In addition, the company offers interior and exterior renovations, remodelling, building additions, ground up construction of new buildings, and other related infrastructures.
