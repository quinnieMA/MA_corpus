This company is engaged in the manufacture and distribution of cigarettes, loose-leaf tobacco, and other tobacco-related products, including filter papers and tubes. It was formerly known as Imasco Ltd. The company has its registered office located in Montreal, Quebec. It was established in 1908. The company manufactures a wide variety of tobacco products under the brand names du MAURIER, Player's, John Player, JPS, Matinee, Peter Jackson, Sweet Caporal, Embassy, Hi-Dro, Zig Zag, Marlboro, Kool, Cameo, and Avanti. Its products are distributed by the following Imperial Tobacco affiliated companies: Imperial Brands Ltd.; John Player & Sons Ltd.; and Allan Ramsay & Company Ltd. The company is Canada's leading tobacco company. Its mission is to compete successfully for market share in any segment it enters, by satisfying adult smoker preferences better than the competition. The company operates a tobacco processing facility in Aylmer, Ontario. It has six sales offices across Canada: Moncton, Atlantic; Pointe-Claire, Quebec; Greater Toronto Area (GTA); Ontario outside of GTA; Calgary, Prairies; and Vancouver, British Columbia. The company also has business operation in the United States.
