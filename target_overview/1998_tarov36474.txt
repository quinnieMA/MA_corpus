This company is engaged in the wholesale of computer and computer peripheral equipment and software, as well as office equipments. It was founded in 1997, and the company's registered office is located in Munchen, Germany. It has two regional departments, in Braunschweig and Lippstadt, as well as a logistics center in Straubing, Germany. The company distributes branded components and peripherals such as keyboards, mouse, speakers, joystick, flash drives, scanners, printers, microphones, Web cams, and monitors. Its software solutions are ERP systems; business applications; network information systems; software analysis and Web page design services.
