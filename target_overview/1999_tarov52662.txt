This company is engaged in the manufacture of balancing and diagnostic systems, assembly and testing systems, filling systems, and industrial cleaning technology. It was founded in 1881 and has its corporate office located in Darmstadt, Germany. The company offers machinery, equipment, systems, and services for the balancing of rotating and oscillating components and assemblies; and testing and diagnostics equipment for automotive manufacturers and supply companies, the aerospace industry, electrical and turbine machine industries, and the general engineering industry. It also provides service facilities ranging from facility management to financial services and medical services to training; solutions for research and development, production, maintenance, and quality control operations; and office premises, production workshops, and warehousing areas. In addition, it offers services ranging from vehicle fleet management to the company restaurant, from training to logistics services; and operates a conference center for conferences and events. The company serves automotive, electrical, aeronautical, aerospace, turbomachinery construction, general mechanical engineering, and other industries. It has manufacturing facilities in Europe, America, and Asia.
