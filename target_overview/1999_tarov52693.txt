This entity is an active holding company whose subsidiaries engaged in heavy building materials and holding company principally in the United Kingdom. It was formerly known as Tarmac Holdings Plc. The company was incorporated in November of 1913. The registered head office of the company is primarily based in Birmingham, West Midlands, United Kingdom. The company's subsidiaries comprise the wholesale distribution of wood, construction materials and sanitary equipment to public and private commercial and industrial business sector clients. It is also engaged in mining and quarrying and in the general construction of buildings and civil engineering works and other business related activities for the most part in the United Kingdom. The company is the shareholder of Tarmac Limited, an active private company formerly known as Tarmac Heavy Building Materials UK Limited and is engaged in the exploiting resources of stone, sand and gravel to satisfy the needs of the building and construction industries for crushed and graded aggregates and operates in the United Kingdom.
