This company operates as a holding firm. It was incorporated in June 1953 and conducts business from its registered head office located in Emerainville, France. The company is wholly owned by Decomeubles Partners.The company specializes in providing financial services and investments. It encompasses a range of activities, including portfolio management, investment advice, and trust services. It administers, manages, and oversees group activities. It assumes a decision-making role, handles administrative affairs, and coordinates services for business units in France and Luxembourg. Additionally, it undertakes organizational planning for its subsidiaries, ensuring efficient financial support and control for the board. Key responsibilities include managing the group's legal structure, tax planning, and financial and equity structures.
