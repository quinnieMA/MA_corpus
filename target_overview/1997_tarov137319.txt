The company, based in the Netherlands, is engaged in the wholesale distribution of a range of compound baked tobacco leaf and other tobacco products. It was incorporated in the year 1990 and has its registered business address located in Joure.Some of the products the company distributes are chewing tobacco, pipe tobacco, smoking tobacco, snuff, cigarettes, imitation tobacco, tobacco product accessories and supplies, and other tobacco products. In addition, the company also exports these products to international markets.
