The company is engaged in the manufacture and sale of catering products. It was established in the year 1826. The registered head office of the company is located in the city of Oldburry, United Kingdom. The company is the largest manufacturer of catering products in the UK. It markets its products under the brand name Longlife and Mermaid. The company offers a number of products such as bakeware, cookware, and utensils. Its bakeware includes aluminum baking pans, stainless steel, pizza pans, and catering bakeware. The cookware of the company consists of ground base aluminum, heavy weight catering cookware, and microwave cookware. In addition, it caters several bar products such as wine coolers and stands, bar accessories, openers, cocktail accessories, bottle trolleys, and ash trays.
