The company is a private holding firm whose subsidiaries are engaged in the provision of printing services in the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in the year 1996 and conducts its business from its registered head office located in Kilmarnock. The company primarily involves in holding the securities of companies and enterprises for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions. It also undertakes the strategic and organizational planning and decision making role of the company and enterprise. The company primarily operates in the United Kingdom. The company is under the management and supervision of Gordon Batten Cunningham, the Director.
