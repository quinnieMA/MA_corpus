The company is engaged in publishing activities. It was incorporated in 1984 and has its registered office in Norwich, United Kingdom. The company's business operation specializes in the publication of paid for and free weekly newspapers and the manufacture and sale of customized envelopes. The group's newspaper publishing activities are concentrated in three publishing centres serving the Greater London area. It also distribute or arrange for the distribution of these works, or may create the works in-house, contract for, purchase, or compile works that were originally created by others. These works may be published in one or more formats, such as print and electronic form, including proprietary electronic networks. The group's envelope manufacturing business serves customers in the South of England.
