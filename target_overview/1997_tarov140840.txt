This company provides management services and corporate support to its subsidiaries in the childcare and sports/fitness sectors. It was incorporated in July 1993 as Premium Leisure (Holdings) Plc. Its registered office is located in London, United Kingdom. The company oversees the operations of the following subsidiaries: Riverside Childcare Limited, which provides experienced and qualified child carers in a wide variety of group settings, including day nurseries, nursery schools, childcare centres and schools in London; Riverside Racquet Centre Limited, which operates racquet clubs in London; and Surrey Tennis & Country Club Limited, the operator of Surrey Tennis & Country Club (STCC).
