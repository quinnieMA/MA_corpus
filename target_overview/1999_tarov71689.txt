This company is engaged in providing high-value analog semiconductor solutions. Its registered office is located in Migdal Haemek, Israel. The company was founded in 1993. The company specializes in Radio Frequency (RF) technologies, High Performance Analog (HPA) solutions, Integrated Power Management systems, CMOS Image Sensors (CIS), Non-Imaging Sensors (NIS), Mixed-Signal CMOS technologies, Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS), Power Discrete components, Analog RF, Power, Displays, and custom analog solutions for differentiated products, along with services like process transfer, design enablement, multi-fab wafer production, in-house manufacturing, comprehensive project management, and operational excellence, supported by global facilities in Israel, the US, Japan, and Italy.
