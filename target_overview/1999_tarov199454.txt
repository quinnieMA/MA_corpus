This company is engaged in the provision of content solutions and electronic publishing technologies for business Web sites and enterprise Intranets principally in the United States. The company was established by Donald McLagan in 1998. The registered head office of the company is located in Burlington, Massachusetts.The company offers technology and services for customers to create, manage, and deploy content for millions of end-users through Intranets, Web sites, portals, Extranets, and distribution channels. The company targets markets for syndicated news solutions through the sale of its Information Products and Content Solutions. Information Products are sold to large organizations for use by their employees. Its Information Products deliver news and information to large numbers of users within organisations through corporate Intranets or local area networks.
