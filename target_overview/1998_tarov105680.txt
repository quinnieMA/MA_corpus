Dennis Group Limited is a private limited company primarily engaged in the design, manufacture and sale of a range of specialist vehicles for United Kingdom and overseas markets. The company's registered office address is located in Coventry, United Kingdom. The company was formerly known as Trinity Holdings plc.Dennis Group Plc is a world leader in niche markets for specialist vehicles.The company's global and domestic ultimate owner is The Mayflower Corporation plc, which is engaged in the design, engineering and manufacture of car bodies and commercial vehicle cabs, vehicle conversions and the production of synthetic webbing.One of the company's subsidiary is Dennis Fire, the United Kingdom's leading fire engine maker. Other subsidiary of the company is Dennis Bus, the United Kingdom's largest bus chassis manufacturer, bus and coach body kit builder.
