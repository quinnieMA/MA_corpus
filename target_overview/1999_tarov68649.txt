The company is a holding firm whose subsidiaries are engaged in the provision of marketing consultancy services. It was formerly known as Dart Sherrington Limited and was incorporated in October of 1988. The company conducts business from its registered head office located in Surrey, United Kingdom.The company, through its subsidiaries, offers brand development and marketing insight services to companies across all industry sectors. It provides services related to the planning, buying and evaluation of advertising campaigns, marketing consultancy and branding, identity and design. The company is comprised of a media communications network, a digital marketing agency, a strategic marketing consultancy, a global branding, identity and design group, a business measuring marketing effectiveness and an integrated lead agency working with all of these disciplines.
