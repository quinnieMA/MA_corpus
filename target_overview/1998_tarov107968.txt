The company is engaged in the provision of refrigeration and service industry machinery. It was established and incorporated in 1980. The registered business office of the company is located in King's Lynn, United Kingdom.The company offers a wide range of products such as gastronorm cabinets and counters, prep stations, blast chillers/freezers, bottle coolers, display cabinets and modular coldrooms. It also specializes in manufacturing dedicated bakery refrigeration and dough conditioning equipment.It is considered one of the market-leading manufacturers and providers of high quality, awarding winning commercial refrigeration solutions meeting global commercial foodservice requirements worldwide.The company is active and operational in the United Kingdom.
