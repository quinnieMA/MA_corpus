The company is a privately held firm that is engaged in the manufacture of electric motors and generators. It was incorporated in May of 1920 and conducts business operations from its corporate head office located in Sheffield, S3 8AQ, United Kingdom. The company's product portfolio includes DC Motors: Brushed DC motors, Brushless DC motors, and Coreless or Ironless DC motors; Universal motors; AC motors; Torque motors; Slip ring; Stepper motors; Linear motors; Doubly-fed electric motor; Singly-fed electric motor; and Nanotube nanomotor. It also offers internal combustion engine starting motors; power generators, including battery charging alternators for internal combustion engines; and motor generator sets such as turbine generator set units. These products are used in various household appliances such as fans, refrigerators, washing machines, pool pumps, floor vacuums, clothes dryers, hair dryers, forced air home furnaces, computers requiring cooling, attic fans, range hoods, bathroom fans, boot dryers, pellet stoves, electric heaters, heat guns, and fan-forced ovens.
