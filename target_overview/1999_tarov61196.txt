Cendris 1 Limited is a privately held company which provides data and document management services. Cendris 1 was incorporated in April 1970 and does business from its registered corporate office located in Bury, Lancashire, United Kingdom. Cendris 1 operates as a wholly owned subsidiary of Cendris UK Limited and is globally owned by TNT N.V. TNT provides businesses and consumers worldwide with an extensive range of services for their mail and express delivery needs. TNT serves more than 200 countries and employs more than 161,500 people. Over 2007, TNT reported 11 billion Euros in revenues and an operating income of 1,192 million Euros.
