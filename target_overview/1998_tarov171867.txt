The company is engaged in the exploitation of copper deposits and other mine pits for the glass, ceramic, refractory and metallurgy industries. Its business was established in April of 1900, and was formerly known as Otavi Minen und Eisenbahn-Gesellschaft. The company has a registered head office based in Eschborn/Taunus, Germany.The company offers products such as barium carbonate, calcined bauxit, brown fused alumina, chamotte, lithium carbonate, pyrophyllite, spodumene, steel fibres, thermocarbon, and wollastonite. The company also supplies metallurgy raw materials for various applications such as welding consumer goods, foundry additives, and continuous casting powder.The company also builds a railway in Namibia.
