This company is engaged in the production and distribution of bottled natural mineral water and other specialty beverages. The company has its registered head office in Bruxelles, Belgium. It was incorporated in 1980.The company is a Belgian international group centered on the production and marketing of natural mineral waters under a wide range of brands and in the production of other soft drinks and fruit cordials. The company also runs a thermal bathing center and a research laboratory in Hydro Geology. It is one of the top-ranking firm in Belgium and the Benelux market's leading supplier of bottled water. The company operates under the Spadel Group which is also active in the production of bottled mineral water. The Group is also involved in the operation of subsidiaries in the Netherlands, Germany, the United Kingdom and France. The Group's brands include Spa, Spontin and Bru in Belgium, Wattwiler in France and Brecon in the UK.
