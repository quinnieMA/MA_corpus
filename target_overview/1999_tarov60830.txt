This company is engaged in the provision of aviation ground services. It was incorporated in 1967 and conducts its business from its corporate office in Runcorn, United Kingdom with locations in Asia, Canada, Europe, South America, the United Kingdom, Ireland, and the United States. The company offers ground handling services, such as passenger and ramp/technical services, passenger check-in, ticketing, boarding, lost and found, irregularity handling, security screening, baggage loading, pushback, towing, repositioning, aircraft cleaning, toilet/water, aircraft de-icing, load control, communications, flight operations, dispatch, line and GSE maintenance, and supervision. It offers cargo processing services, such as cargo warehousing, aircraft loading and unloading, trucking, documentation, dangerous goods handling, customs brokerage, freight forwarding, supervision, and capacity control; and into-plane fuelling services, such as fuel management for airlines in the procurement of fuel and fuel related services.
