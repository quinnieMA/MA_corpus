This company, with registered head office located in Villanueva de Gallego, Spain, is engaged in the distribution of pharmaceutical products primarily in Spain. The company comprises 13 operating companies which are all active in pharmaceutical distribution. The company was founded in 1919 as Sociedad Anonima Farmaceutica Aragonesa (SAFA) by a group of pharmacists from Zaragosa. It is one of the leaders in the pharmaceutical distribution industry in Spain. It has market operations in the Teruel, Huesca, Tarragona, Zaragoza, and Segovia provinces and it has more than 30 stores that distributes to approximately 9,000 pharmacies.
