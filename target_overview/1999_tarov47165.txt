This company is engaged in the fire safety and fire suppression industries. The company was incorporated in 1934. The registered head office of the company is located in Guildford, United Kingdom. The company specialises in providing fire extinguisher servicing, supply, and installation; kitchen fire suppression systems such as Ansul R-102 and Amerex systems; and fire risk assessments, along with fire safety training. Its mission is to safeguard lives and property through expert fire safety solutions, delivering reliable fire extinguisher servicing, cutting-edge kitchen fire suppression systems, and comprehensive fire risk assessments and training. The company's commitment to excellence, safety, and innovation in the field of fire safety and suppression aligns with the company's core services and expertise.
