This company is engaged in the wholesale distribution of building materials. It was incorporated in 1971. The registered head office of the company is located in Paris Cedex, France. The company provides a range of building materials such as covering floors & walls, bathroom furniture, ceiling tiles, adhesives, locks, fasteners, timbers, nails, electrical supplies, plumbing fixtures and supplies, wall panels, screens welded items, cement products and other related products. It caters to construction companies, home improvement contractors, and others. The company conducts its business activities throughout France and further serves the general public of the city, as well as the surrounding areas.
