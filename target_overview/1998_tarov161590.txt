The company is a UK-based privately held firm that is engaged in the manufacture of perfumes and toilet preparations. It was incorporated in December of 1990. The registered business office of the company is strategically located in St Albans, United Kingdom.The company's principal activity is primarily engaged in preparing, blending, compounding, and packaging toilet preparations, such as perfumes, shaving preparations, hair preparations, face creams, and other cosmetic preparations. The company is affiliated with Pricewaterhousecoopers Llp., which acts as its auditor.
