The company is engaged in manufacturing and trading of engineering components. It was previously known as Brooke Tool Engineering (Holdings) Public Limited Company. The company has a registered head office based in Balm Green (Sheffield) in the United Kingdom.The company operates in four divisions: cutting tools, which offers circular saws and cutters; carbide, which supplies carbide wear parts; springs such as coil, shutter door springs and hot coiling; and metal parts including metals and woven wires. It also offers metalworking machine tools, cutting tool and machine tool accessories, as well as fabricated metal products.The company utilizes high technology equipment and machinery to ensure high quality products and services. It always partners with its clients to provide total customer satisfaction and have continuous growth and development.
