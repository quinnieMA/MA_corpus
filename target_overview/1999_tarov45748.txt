The company is primarily engaged in the provision of telecommunication services. The registered head office of the company is located in Rome, Italy.The company provides a wide range of telecommunication services, such as fibre-optic networks and the installation of fixed and mobile phones for commercial and private use. The company is also involved in other activities, such as internet applications and satellite network; information technology and on-line services; outscoring, system integration and telematic services; and engineering and manufacturing telecommunication products. In August of 2003, the company was absorbed by Olivetti S.p.A.
