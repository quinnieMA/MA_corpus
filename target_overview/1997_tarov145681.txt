The company is a privately held holding firm which is engaged in management activities of holding companies. It was incorporated in October of 1994 and was formerly known as Broomco (838) Limited. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Chatham, United Kingdom.As a holding firm, the company primarily provides financial support and control function for the board, and is responsible for managing the group and its overall legal structure, tax planning, financial and sequity structures. It also handles the administrative affairs and services of a holding company and grants management services to its subsidiaries. In addition, it is involved in matters of policy, strategic planning, marketing, selecting and manning senior management positions, approving investments and budgets, and the overall ongoing monitoring of the group companies' performance.
