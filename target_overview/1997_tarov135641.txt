The firm is a private holding company whose group is engaged in the provision of business support and management services principally in the United Kingdom. The company was incorporated in the year 1993. It was formerly known as Teamring Ltd which existed until November 1993. The registered head office of the company is located in United Kingdom.The firm comprises its business operation in the management activities of holding companies and other related enterprises in the United Kingdom. The company operates under the supervision and management of Rupert Nicholas Hambro, Chairman of the Board.
