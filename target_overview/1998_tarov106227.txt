The company is engaged in the development, sale and lease of real estate properties. The company is also involved in the management of real estate on a fee and/or contract basis. The company, formerly known as BDA Holdings plc, was incorporated in 1973. The registered head office of the company is located in London, United Kingdom.The company specializes in the promotion, sales and lease of houses and the construction of flats. It also serves as operators of apartments, such as single and multi family, studios and flats, and residential mobile home sites. Lastly, the company also manages non-residential buildings and other kinds of properties.
