The company is primarily engaged in the wholesale trade of electrical and electronic goods. Incorporated in May of 1980, it was formerly known as C.E.M. Micro-Computer Services Limited. The company has a registered office located in Belfast in Northern Ireland, United Kingdom.The company distributes and sells a wide range of electrical and electronic goods, including electric alarm apparatus; batteries; circuit breakers; coaxial cables; electrical construction materials; current-carrying wiring devices; electrical distribution equipment; electric motors, wiring supplies and lighting fixtures; flashlights; electric fuses; electrical generators; transmission pool and line hardware; electrical industrial controls; insulated wire and cable; electrical insulators; light bulbs; electrical lugs and connectors; electric meters; electric motor controls; and panelboards.The company is a subsidiary of Unicomp Inc., an international computer-consulting leader worldwide. Unicomp provides a full range of computer-consulting services, including computer training, system engineering, computer programming, system architecture, and total system solutions.
