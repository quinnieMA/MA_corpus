The company is engaged in the provision of mobile broadband multimedia and interactive services. It provides network for private and business wireless telephone services at home and abroad. In addition, the company provides technical support services and replacement sets, as well as constant upgrading of the network to improve quality and territorial coverage. The company was incorporated in 1995. Its registered business office address is located in Rome in Italy. The company is also active in Brazil, Peru, Venezuela, and Greece. The company is a technology leader and a trailblazer in a customer-centric approach. Its strengths are in high value-added broadband mobile services, and sales packages tailored to the needs of specific customer target groups, from residential customers to businesses, young people and professionals. The company has tightly segmented its domestic market into packages tailored for every customer group, from residential users (TIM Famiglia) to the youth market (TIM Trib- package), the mass market (TIM Club), and professionals (integrated solutions under the One office solutions package). The company's innovation-centric focus is self-evident in the many high-tech products and services that the company has been first to bring to market, from MMS to Mobile TV. The company continues to develop high value-added services, with a focus on the new frontiers of open web browsing and user-generated content. It offers a wide range of mobile broadband multimedia and interactive services via 3G cell phones. The company also offers the widest phone portfolio on the Italian market, featuring 42 exclusive models (of which 34 are UMTS phones). The company has joined forces with Orange, T-Mobile and Telia Soneras as part of FreeMove, one of the world's largest mobile telephony alliances with 295 million customers in 28 nations worldwide.
