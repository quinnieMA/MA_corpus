The company, based in the United Kingdom, operates as a holding company whose subsidiaries are engaged in the manufacture of wood based products such as particle board, medium density fiber board and oriented strand board for the home improvements, furniture and industrial construction board markets. It is a privately held company incorporated in 1980 and was formerly known as CSC Forest Products Limited. Its registered business address located in South Molton.The company is involved in holding the securities of its subsidiaries for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms. In addition, it is also responsible in the provision of a range of corporate services for its subsidiaries including the areas of financing, investment, legal affairs, tax affairs and other related services.
