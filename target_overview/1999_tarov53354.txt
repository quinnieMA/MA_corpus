This company engaged in the wholesale distribution of petroleum products. It is also known as CGPP. It was established in 1938. The registered business office of the company is located in Puteaux, France. The company offers a wide range of products such as liquid butane, propane gas, petroleum products, and other related products. It also provides energy needs for heating, cooking, hot water and processes. It has approximately 420 service stations that distribute LPG. It also operates 18 local branches, as well as 1,000 collaborators. The company is a major distributor in other European countries, including Spain, Belgium, the Netherlands and the Scandinavian countries. Some of the company's listed subsidiaries are SHV Gas Supply & Risk Management and Liquigas S.p.A. These subsidiaries are involved in the manufacture and wholesale distribution of petroleum products.
