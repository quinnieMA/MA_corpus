This company operates as a holding firm engaged in the management activities of holding companies. The company was incorporated in 1934, and it conducts business from its registered head office located in Trelleborg, Sweden. As a holding company, it is also responsible for managing the group and its overall legal structure, tax planning, financial and equity structures as well as involved in matters of policy, strategic planning, marketing, selecting and manning senior management positions, approving investments and budgets, and the overall ongoing monitoring of the group companies' performance.
