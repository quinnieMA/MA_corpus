The company, formerly known as Fife Indmar Plc, is a United Kingdom-based company engaged in the wholesale and retail distribution of ironmongery and industrial hardware and the provision of engineering products, components and services. The company was incorporated in February 1913. Its registered business office is based in Glasgow, United Kingdom. The company operates its business with a legal form as Public, Not Quoted. It also operates with a local registry filing as its filing type.
