This company is engaged in the manufacture and fabrication of rolled steel products. It was incorporated in October of 1981 and has its registered office in Marchienne-Au-Pont, Belgium. The company has state-of-the-art technology and equipment for manufacturing, as well as employs certified staff skilled in manufacturing operations in compliance with the standards and other technical specifications. It operates as a subsidiary of Industeel France S.A., which researches, designs, and manufactures steel products.
