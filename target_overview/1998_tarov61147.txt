The company is a holding company for a group engaged in the development and wholesale of highly integrated camera microchip products. The company, formerly known as Vision Group plc, was incorporated in 1995. The registered head office of the company is located in Edinburgh, United Kingdom. The company exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top management, as well as provides central services. The company is also active in holding the securities of companies and enterprises to own a controlling interest. In addition, the company operates as an establishment that administers, manages, and oversees other establishments of the company or enterprise in the United Kingdom.
