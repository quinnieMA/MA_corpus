The company offers oil and gas extraction, exploration, and production services in the Midcontinent and offshore Gulf of Mexico in the United States, and internationally in the United Kingdom North Sea, Indonesia, China, and Algeria. It operates as an oil company formed in 1966 through the merger of East Coast-based Atlantic Refining and the Californian-based Richfield Petroleum. The company is officially known as BP West Coast Products LLC. It is headquartered in La Palma, California in the United States.The company is the seventh largest petroleum/chemical company in America and operates under two business segments: exploration and production; and refining and marketing. The company focused its upstream exploration and production operations in Alaska, the Gulf of Mexico and the Midcontinent in the United States, the United Kingdom North Sea, Indonesia, Venezuela, China and Algeria. The oil production in Alaska is integrated with the ARCO's downstream refining and marketing operations in the west part of the United States. These operations consist of two refineries, branded consumer marketing outlets in six western states and British Columbia, a marine fleet and supporting pipelines and terminals.The company's patented gasoline has CleanTech content, a cleaning additive. Its gasoline stations are located in five western states of the US including Arizona, California, Nevada, Oregon and in Washington. The company trades under the ARCO brand.
