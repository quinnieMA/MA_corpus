This company is engaged in the manufacture and supply of ceramics and associated raw materials for the tableware, sanitary ware, and tile industries. It was incorporated in 1994 and has registered headquarters located in London, the United Kingdom. The company offers a comprehensive portfolio of raw materials and additives for glaze preparation; prepared coloured and transparent glazes in slop, dry and composite forms; prepared clay bodies in slip, pressed cake and pugged forms; frits; glaze and body stains; and decorative on-glazes, and zircon opacifiers. Its products are sold across the UK through a network of local dealers. The company is a subsidiary of Johnson Matthey PLC, a global specialty chemicals company that focuses on catalysis, precious metals, fine chemicals and process technology.
