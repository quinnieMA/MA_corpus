The company is engaged in manufacturing and sale of a wide variety of disk storage devises, printers, monitors, input/output devices and terminals for the computer industry. Its registered head office is strategically located in Avranches Cedex, France. The company's products include removable storage devices such as CD/DVD burners, floppy drives, zip drives, and disks and accessories for zip; data recovery devices for hard drive, server, removable media, tape backup, flash/USB thumb drive data, and application and database recovery; digital storage drives and disks, network attached storage products, desktop hard drives, portable hard drives, network hard drives, network storage racks, and external hard drives; screenplay, DVD/CDRW, and clearance multimedia drives; and backup drives and removable disks, server backup kits, and disks and accessories. Its products are marketed and sold throughout France through its network of distributors in the country.
