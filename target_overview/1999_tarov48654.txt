The company is engaged in the provision of high quality print, direct mail and design services to businesses in the United Kingdom. The company was founded in 1978 and its registered head office is located in Manchester, United Kingdom.The company offers poster size printing, black and white copying, color copying, design, digital black and white printing, plan copying, and digital color printing services, as well as prints business stationery, marketing and sales collateral, HR and training literature, banner stands and exhibition material using the latest digital printing equipment and graphics design software.The company is a member of British Franchise Association, the voluntary self regulating governing body for franchising in the United Kingdom market.The company is a subsidiary of United Kingdom-based Adare Group, a leading, international provider of marketing and customer communications solutions, offering all the components that make up an end-to-end marketing and communications strategy.
