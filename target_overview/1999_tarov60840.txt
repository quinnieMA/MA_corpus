Cirqual Limited is a UK-based company that operates as a group that manufacture, anodises and fabricates aluminium extrusions as well as design and produces complex extruded thermoplastic profiles. The company was established in 1994 and has its registered office in Ashbourne, United Kingdom. It was formerly known as Danepace Limited. Cirqual Limited designs and manufactures specialist components for various industries. The group comprises of three divisions: Building components, Telecommunications components and Aerospace components. The Building components division supplies extrusions to manufacturing industries which include shopfitting, lighting, display casing, domestic appliances, automotive, aerospace and packaging. The Telecommunications components division concentrates on the manufacture of multi-layer printed circuit boards. The Aerospace components division provides machining and metal fabrication services to the aerospace and automotive industries. Cirqual Limited provides specialist engineering service principally to metal fabrication and machining to the aerospace, automotive, and electronic markets.
