The intermediate holding company has interests in other companies active in the wholesale sector. It was incorporated in October 1977 and has its head office located in Birmingham, United Kingdom.The company holds investments in other companies engaged in the distribution of carpets and rugs in the UK. It also offers legal, supervisory, human resource guidance and support services to its subsidiary undertakings. Within the framework of its financial ability, it provides funding for other business firms. In addition, the company renders risk management and consultancy services, delivering a broad array of solutions focusing on business strategic planning. The company predominantly operates within the domestic sector.
