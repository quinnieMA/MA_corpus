The company, based in the United Kingdom, is engaged in the retail distribution of a range of shoes, luggage and other leather goods. It is a privately held company incorporated in 1994 and was formerly known as Capolito Roma Group Public Limited Company. Its registered business address is located in the city of London.The company is involved in the distribution of rubber and plastics footwear with vulcanized rubber soles, molded or cemented to rubber, plastics, or fabric uppers, and rubber protective footwear. Some of the products the company distributes are leather sandals, leather boots, rubber water shoes, rubber athletic shoes, attache cases, athletic bags, briefcases, luggage cases, valises, trunks, suitcases, traveling bags, and wardrobe bags.
