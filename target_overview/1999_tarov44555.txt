Formerly known as Kasha Technology Limited, this company is engaged in the provision of management services to the other Microgen group companies. It was incorporated in October 1995 and has its registered office located in London, the United Kingdom.The company has team of professional staff and other specialists with expertise in providing necessary assistance and services to its clients. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Microgen PLC, a leading IT services and solutions group providing consultancy, managed services, and software solutions.
