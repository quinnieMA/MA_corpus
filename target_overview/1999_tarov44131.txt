This company is engaged in the operation of a supermarket chain. It was founded by Herb Brody, Milton Perlmutter and Alex Aidekman in 1968. The registered head office of the company is located in Montvale, United States of America. The company offers assortment of foods and general merchandise, as well as a host of additional conveniences, including 128 in-store, full-service pharmacies and an array of financial services offered by 71 in-store banks. Majority of the company's stores are located in New York, New Jersey metro area and 29 of its stores are in the greater Philadelphia metro area. Pathmark was a pioneer in the super center concept, which combined traditional grocery and perishable items with expanded offerings on non-foods, including small appliances. It was the first supermarket company in the Northeast to operate its stores 24 hours each day, 7 days a week. Furthermore, the company was among the first in the Northeast to utilize scanning registers at the checkout.
