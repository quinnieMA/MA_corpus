The privately-held company, formerly known as Unicorn International PLC, is engaged in the production and supply of a wide range of abrasive products. Incorporated in November 1991, it operates from its head office located in Staffordshire, United Kingdom.The company is one of the world's leading manufacturers of abrasive products. It serves a wide client base, including the automotive, aerospace, engineering, and construction industries. Its products are sold across the UK through a network of local distributors and sales partners.The company operates as a subsidiary of Saint-Gobain, a market leading producer and distributor of materials for the construction, industrial and consumer sectors.
