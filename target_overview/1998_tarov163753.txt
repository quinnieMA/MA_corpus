This company, with headquarters in Newcastle Upon Tyne, United Kingdom, engaged in providing airport services. It was formerly known as GHI Limited. The company was incorporated in 1971. The company is Go-Ahead's aviation services company. Go-Ahead adopted the company's name following the acquisition of Reed Aviation, Midland Airport Services, and British Midland Airways' ground handling activities, which merged with the Go-Ahead-owned Gatwick Handling (GHI). The company serves 17 airports across the United Kingdom and Ireland, including London Heathrow, London Gatwick, Birmingham, Manchester, and Dublin. It offers a wide range of services, including passenger processing, information desks, ticket services, business lounges, baggage handling, cargo handling, cargo customs clearance, and, of course, aircraft coordination. The company is part of the much larger Aviance International Alliance. Set up in 2000, Aviance International Alliance incorporates the local expertise of services at 77 airports in 11 countries, including Turkey, Spain, Switzerland, America, Argentina, and Israel. In August 2004, the company acquired full ownership of Plane Handling Limited, purchasing the remaining 50 percent shareholding from its then joint venture partner Virgin Aviation. As part of this transaction, Virgin Aviation has also entered into a new five-year contract with the company and Plane Handling for the cargo handling and ground handling services at London Heathrow and Manchester and the cargo handling at London Gatwick and Glasgow airports. The company is a subsidiary of Go-Ahead Group, the largest commuter rail operator in the capital.
