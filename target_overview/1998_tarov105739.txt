The company, formerly known as James Dickie & Company (Drop Forgings) Plc, operates as a privately held holding firm. It was incorporated in October of 1936. The registered head office of the company is strategically located in Glasgow, United Kingdom. The company handles the administrative affairs and services of a holding company and grants management services to its subsidiaries. It is also responsible in managing its subsidiaries and its overall legal structure, tax planning, financial and equity structures. The company's subsidiaries are involved in mechanical engineering activities. It principally conducts its business operations within the United Kingdom. The company operates as a subsidiary of Eliza Tinsley Group Plc, an international engineering business organized into off-highway and consumer products divisions.
