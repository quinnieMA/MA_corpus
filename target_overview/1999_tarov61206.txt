Content Beheer NV, incorporated in 1964, is a Dutch-based employment company. The company specialises in recruitment and selection of permanent and temporary staff for the most varied positions demanding both pre and post-college competence in the administrative, secretarial and commercial fields, and in the world of computerisation and callcenters. In addition, the company also provides career planning, training, data-entry and automation services.Content Beheer serves the staffing needs of many sectors from ICT and banking to insurance and business. The company also contributes essential man and woman power to government and (semi-) government organisations such as ministries and municipalities. Besides recruitment and selection, secondment and deployment, Content Beheer is a mediator for multilinguals and non-Dutch speakers.Content Beheer is part of Solvus Resource Group, a Belgian company operating a network of recruitment agencies in many European countries and based in Belgium. The company's head office is located in Den Haag, Netherlands.
