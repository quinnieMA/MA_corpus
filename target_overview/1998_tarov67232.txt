This company is engaged in the manufacture of refined petroleum products. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Chicago, United States of America. The company was founded in 1909. The company offers oil and natural gas exploration, field development, and production; midstream transportation, storage, and processing; and marketing and trading of natural gas, including liquefied natural gas (LNG), and power and natural gas liquids (NGLs). It also provides crude oil, petroleum, and petrochemical products. In addition, the company is involved in the exploration and production of hydrocarbons, as well as jet fuel, bunkering, bitumen, and lubricants activities. The company is formerly known as Standard Oil (Indiana).
