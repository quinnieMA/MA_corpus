This company is engaged as wholesalers of central heating and plumbing equipment. It was incorporated in 1988. It was formerly known as PTS Group PLC until January 2000. As a holding company, it is involved in the management of its enterprises through holding the securities or other equity interests for the purpose of owning a controlling interest or influencing the management decisions of its subsidiaries. The company's subsidiary, PTS Plumbing Trade Supplies, distributes a comprehensive range of bathroom, heating and plumbing products to a wide range of customers, both public and private sector, from national house builders to the sole trading plumber. PTS Plumbing markets BOSS and Tradesave brands. Other subsidiaries of the company are Tricom Group Limited and Fry & Pollard Ltd. Its headquarters in Northampton, United Kingdom.
