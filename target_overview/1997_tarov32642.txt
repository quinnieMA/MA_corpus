This company is engaged in the generation of energy for electricity services in Germany. It was founded in 1884 by Emil Rathenau and Oskar von Miller. It has its registered office located in Berlin, Germany. The company is jointly controlled by Bayernwerk together with Southern Energy, an equity investment company belonging to the American Southern Company. The company does not possess any base load power stations and therefore has to purchase base load electricity. Since 1996, the company has been responsible for supplying energy in the form of electricity and heat to the whole of Berlin. The company works in partnership and cooperation with a number of the power supply companies both in Germany and abroad. As Berlin's premier energy supplier, the company also provides the city's districts with trouble shooting advice and general maintenance support services. It also provides comprehensive consulting helpline service which includes the provision of comprehensive complete system solutions promoting greater energy efficiency.
