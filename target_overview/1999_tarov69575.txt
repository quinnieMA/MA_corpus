This company is engaged in the manufacture and distribution of cement products in Belgium. It was incorporated in 1929 and has its registered head office located in Braine-L' Alleud, Belgium. The company's production sites are located in Lixhe, Antoing, Ghent, and Harmignies. The company produces and markets a wide range of cement products such as white cements, metallurgic cements, and special cements. It is a white cement producer in Benelux which offers decorative, white or colored mortars and concrete in a non-aggressive environment such as Flashwhite CEM II/A-LL 42,5 N; Blancbel CEM 42,5 N LA; Blancbel CEM 52,5 R LA; and Blancbel CEM 52,5 N LA. Its clients include RC Grouppe Ronveaux, Ytong, Rodal, and VCR Van Cauwenbergh. The company also markets a whole range of Sika Addiment admixtures, as well as providing technical advices. Its addiment products are available in different types of packaging. Its liquid additives products are delivered in bulk containers, barrels, buckets and cans. Its dry products in powder form are delivered in bags on pallets. Some of the company's products are BV Plasticisers, FM Superplasticisers, LP Air Entrainers, DM Sealants, VZ Retarders, BE Accelerators, TR Release Agents, NB Curing Products, and UW Underwater Compound. The company's main customers are concrete plants, manufacturers of concrete materials, and construction materials distribution chains in Belgium, The Netherlands, and Rhineland-Westphalia in Germany.
