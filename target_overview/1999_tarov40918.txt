This company is engaged in the production, marketing and selling of sugar. It involves in all stages of production from selection of the individual sugar beet, to extraction of the sugar, its packaging and its distribution to all regions of Chile. The company also offers natural fruit juices, frozen vegetables and fruit, farm supplies, and cattle and horse feeds. It produces tomato-based products; fruit-juice concentrates from apple, pear, peach, prune, kiwi, and red raspberry; and inputs for beet production, such as fertilizers, mono products, seeds, and agro chemicals. The company operates five plants, located in Curico, Linares, Chillan, Los Angeles and Rapaco, as well as a packaging plant in Santiago. It also has production facilities in Peru which is involved in the fruit-juice concentrates business jointly with Cargill, an apple juice exporter in the southern hemisphere. It also has a technology transfer agreement with Cargill Animal Nutrition in cattle feed and participates in the frozen vegetable business with the Bonduelle brand. Other brands include Iansa, Patagonia, Agromas and Huertos de Iansa, among others. The company distributes its products in Chile, the United States, Europe, Japan, and Latin America. It was incorporated in 1953 and is based in Santiago, Chile.
