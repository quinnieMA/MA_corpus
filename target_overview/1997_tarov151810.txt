The company, formerly known as Framleycove Limited, is engaged in the management activities of a holding company in the United Kingdom. It is a private limited company which was incorporated in July of 1995. The registered head office of the company is located in Tewkesbury, United Kingdom.The company provides group planning and strategy and in particular exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top managements, and provides central services. It also holds the securities and other equity interests of companies and enterprises for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms. The company's subsidiaries are involved in the manufacture of printed circuit boards (PCBs).
