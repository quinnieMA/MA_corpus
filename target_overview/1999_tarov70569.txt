This company, a unit of ABP-PGGM Capital Holding NV, is primarily engaged in the provision of private equity and mezzanine capital to middle market companies in Western Europe. It conducts business from its registered head office located in Naarden, the Netherlands. The company focuses on participations in unlisted companies based in Benelux and Germany. It typically invests in young companies with very high growth potential, largely in the High-tech sectors. The company's scope of business involves the provision of financing and risk management services to its participations. The company has invested in Leonardo Media BV, the World leader in Travel e-Content Distribution, with more than 16.000 travel distribution channels, such as tour operators and Internet sites.
