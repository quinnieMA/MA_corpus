It is a private company engaged in the manufacture of plastic materials, synthetic resins and non-vulcanizable elastomers. The company was formed as a result of the fusion of Neste and Statoil in 1994. The company's registered main office is located in Kongens Lyngby, Denmark.The company is a leading, global supplier of PE and PP plastic product solutions for the pipe industry. Its portfolio covers products for water and gas distribution systems, coating of steel pipelines, waste and sewage pipe systems, in-house hot and cold water systems, floor heating, chemical and industrial systems, fittings and valves. The company is also a leading provider of polyolefin compounds for the wire and cable industry. Applications are found in building and automotive wires, data and communication cables, and in high, medium and low voltage energy transmission and distribution cables. Furthermore, the company supplies a wide range of plastics products to the automotive industry that are used for dashboards, front ends, air vent systems and bumpers. Also, the company produces polyolefins products that are used as packaging material for applications in healthcare, courier bags, food packaging, flexible and rigid transport packaging, bottles, crates, boxes, trays, large containers and pallets.The company operates with a number of partners in Europe, South America and the Middle East. Its partners are Borouge Pte. Ltd., Speciality Polymers Antwerp, Borealis Brasil S.A., Noretyl, and Borouge (Abu Dhabi Polymers Company Ltd.).The company has European production plants located in Austria, Germany, Italy, Belgium, Finland, Norway, and Sweden. It also has production facilities in the United States of America and Brazil. All the Company's manufacturing units are certified according to ISO 9001. The plants that focus on raw materials for the automotive industry are additionally certified according to ISO/TS 16949.
