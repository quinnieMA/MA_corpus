This holding company is a group engaged in the provision of professional staffing services. The company, incorporated in 1983, was formerly known as Select Appointments (Holdings) Limited. The registered head office of the company is located in Luton, United Kingdom. The company offers on-site recruitment and workforce management services, managed service programs and recruitment process outsourcing solutions, search and selection services, and delivery solutions. It specialises in construction and property, education, social care, engineering, accountancy and finance, information technology, financial service, sale, secretarial and admin, health, recruitment, and customer service recruitment areas. The company's subsidiaries primarily operate in Australia and the United Kingdom. It operates as a sbsidiary of Randstad Holding NV.
