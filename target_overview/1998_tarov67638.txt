This company, the American subsidiary of France-based Alcatel-Lucent S.A., is engaged in the design, development, and building of communications networks. The company offers software applications, equipment, and other related services to network service providers and telecommunications carriers, as well as government customers. It has pioneered the development of digital subscriber line (DSL) technology for residential and small business end-users. The company was incorporated in 1976 and has a registered business address located in Plano, Texas. The company has grown to become a leading supplier of broadband Internet access products. The company's carrier business group include wireless, wireline, and the convergence services. The service business group offers consultation and design, integration and deployment, as well as operation and maintenance. Alcatel's enterprise group delivers communications solutions for businesses, including contact centers. Its portfolio includes products, software and services designed for sharing multimedia information. These business groups develop end-to-end solutions targeted at key market growth segments including carrier IP, IMS, broadband access, network transformation, 3G wireless, video, optical, next-generation applications and other related services.
