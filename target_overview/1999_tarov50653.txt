The company, based in the United Kingdom, is engaged in the management and operation of health and fitness club. The company, with registered head office located in Hatfield, was incorporated in 1995.The company, through its subsidiaries, owns, operates and manages multi-use sports and athletic, professional health and fitness clubs and wellness centers in the United Kingdom. It also offers a wide range of sports and health development programmes, as well as other amenities and services, including fitness studio, sports hall, racquets facilities, exercise machines, health and beauty spas, swimming pool, club lounges with free Internet access, nurseries and specialist sports shops.
