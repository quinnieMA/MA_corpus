This company is engaged in providing cruise industry services and was formerly known as Vard AS. It was incorporated in 1966. The registered business office of the company is located in Oslo, Norway. The company specialises in offering cruise vacations to a wide range of destinations worldwide. It offers various cruise packages, onboard experiences, dining options, entertainment, shore excursions, and accommodations. The company focuses on global expansion to reach new markets, invests in employee development for enhanced service quality, engages in community initiatives, and seeks financial growth through strategic business endeavors. Its aim is to strengthen brand recognition, ensure safety and security onboard, and remain adaptable to evolving market dynamics and customer preferences within the cruise industry.
