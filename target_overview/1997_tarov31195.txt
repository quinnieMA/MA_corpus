This company, domiciled in Santiago, Chile, operates as a holding firm that owns and operates electricity generation, transmission and distribution companies in Chile, Argentina, Brazil, Colombia and Peru. The existence of the company under its current name dates back in August 1988 when Compagna Chilena Metropolitana de Distribucion Electrica S.A. changed its name to Enersis S.A. It is an electric utility company primarily engaged, through its subsidiaries and affiliates, in the generation and distribution of electricity in Chile, Argentina, Colombia and Peru, and in the generation, transmission and distribution of electricity in Brazil. Enersis is one of the largest private sector electricity companies in South America in terms of consolidated assets and operating revenues, with over 11.2 million customers, and the largest electricity company in Chile. Through Endesa Chile, Enersis is one of the largest private sector electricity generation companies in South America in terms of installed capacity. It also has smaller operations in other non-electricity businesses.
