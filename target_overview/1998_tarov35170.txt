This company is engaged in the operation of a public commission for the distribution of electric energy. It was founded in 1962 and has its registered head office located in Fortaleza, Brazil. The company's activities include the transmission, distribution, sale and supply of electric energy for public, industrial and private consumption. It is also active in the research, studies, planning, construction and exploration of the system of electrical power distribution. The company is involved in transforming urban environments through smart city initiatives and sustainable infrastructure. Its offers a range of customer services, including online account management, direct debit options, and support for energy-related inquiries and issues.
