The American-based company is engaged in the design, manufacture, and assemble of industrial machineries and equipment with emphasis on packaging solutions. It was founded in 1905 by Dr. Ruel Anderson Jones as a manufacturer of a novelty items. It has its registered office in Ft Mitchell, Kentucky.The company offers a wide array of machineries such as cartoners, pouch or horizontal form fill & seal, beverage equipment packaging, as well as robotics equipment. These machineries are widely used in various applications such as single or twin bag cartoning, tissue cartons manufacturing, paperboard multi-pack solution (for a wide variety of products including cans, plastic and glass bottles, cups, bowls, and tubs). Some machineries are also used in flexible pouching applications which include side seal, gusseted, stand up, zipper and product feeding & dosing. The company's flexipro robotic picking cell equipment can sort, collate, orient, and place products in a precise manner and handles a wide array of products.It is recognized as the industry leader in high speed horizontal form fill and seal equipment. The company is part of the Oystar Group, one of the world's largest suppliers of packaging machinery.
