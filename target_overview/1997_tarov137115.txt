This company is engaged in the production of a wide range of components and assemblies for aero and marine gas turbines, aircraft landing gear, static gas generators, nuclear and specialist engineering applications. It was incorporated in 1949 and has its registered office based in Birmingham, United Kingdom with additional facilities in Leicester and Alcester.The company is a premier supplier of performance critical parts to the global aerospace, industrial gas turbine and nuclear industries. It primarily focuses on aero engines, landing gear, and industrial gas turbines. It specializes in machining high complexity/critical components to aero, industrial and marine gas turbine, nuclear, and specialist engineering manufacturers. The company offers a range of sheet metal components, fabrications, and assemblies that include combustion liners, transition pieces, fabricated/machined seals, nozzle guide vane inserts, baffles and impingement plates, small pressed parts, turbine cases, machined seals, and turbine discs in a range of exotic materials for performance-critical gas turbine combustion and cooling applications. It also provides components, assemblies, and kits that include torque link assemblies, twisted links, and articulated links, as well as various other structural components and assemblies for commercial and military aircraft landing gear applications; and machined components to the nuclear submarine market.
