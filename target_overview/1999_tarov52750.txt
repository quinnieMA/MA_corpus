The company is engaged in the manufacture of agricultural and construction equipment. It was incorporated in 1999. The registered business office of the company is located in Racine, Wisconsin. The company is considered as a leading manufacturer of agricultural tractors and combines and construction equipment in the United States, as well as one of the industry's largest equipment finance operations. It offers a wide range of products such as tractors, harvesters, sprayers, and hay balers, as well as light-industrial and construction equipment including backhoes, excavators, forklifts, mini loaders, and telescopic handlers.The company is a subsidiary of CNH Global N.V., a global, full-line company involved in both the agricultural and construction equipment businesses.
