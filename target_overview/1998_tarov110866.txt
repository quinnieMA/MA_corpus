This company is primarily engaged in Design, manufacture, development and distribution of electronic components and power conversion products. The company was incorporated in October of 1932 and it was formerly known as Astec (BSR) Plc. It has its registered head office located strategically in Brierley Hill, United Kingdom. The company manufactures electronic equipment such as computer equipments, computer supplies and information processing supplies. The company is a wholly-owned subsidiary Emerson Electric Co which is engaged in developing and supplying product technology and delivering engineering services in a range of industrial, commercial and consumer markets. The company has five business segments: The process management segments provide measurement, control and diagnostic capabilities for automated industrial processes producing item. The industrial automation segment brings integrated manufacturing solutions to industries worldwide. The network power segment provides power. The company is present in US, Malaysia, Germany and Mexico. The company's subsidiaries include Astec America Inc which is a majority owned subsidiary and is engaged in manufacturing electronic components; Stourbridge Holdings (U.K.) Limited, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Astec International. The company primarily conducts its business operations within the domestic market, serving the interest of the city's general public as well as the surrounding areas.
