The company, formerly known as Orange PLC, holds various firms involved in the provision of wireless mobile communications services. It was incorporated in October of 1995. The registered business office of the company is located in Bristol, United Kingdom.The company is responsible for strategic planning for the group and budget coordination as well as all major decisions relating to investments. It also supervises the subsidiary companies and collects their financial data for analysis and comparison with targets and budgets. In addition, the company is involved for relations with shareholders, investors and other stakeholders and all related dissemination of information. Further, it undertakes the coordination of various aspects of the subsidiaries' activities, such as reporting, financing and preparation of promotional material, as well as providing supporting work of various kinds.This holding company is audited by Ernst & Young LLP, an industrial company involved in the provision of audit, tax, consulting, and financial advisory services.
