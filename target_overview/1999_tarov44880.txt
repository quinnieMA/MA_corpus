The company, previously known as Ellison Holdings Limited, is recognised for supplying the definitive product the world over - from automotive, aerospace and general engineering industries to manufacturers of the simplest, everyday household goods. It was incorporated in 1957 and has its registered office in Keighley, United Kingdom. The company offers a wide range of products including basic types of rings, rings for radial assembly, rings for compensating axial play, uniform section snap rings, push-on fix/grip snap rings, wire rings, shim and support rings, and special components.
