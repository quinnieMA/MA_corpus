This company is engaged in the development, production, and wholesale distribution of smart refractory solutions. It was founded in 1825. The registered business office of the company is located in Bjuv, Sweden.The company offers refractory bricks, blocks of refractory ceramic material used in lining furnaces, kilns, fireboxes, and fireplaces.The company is active and operational in Sweden.It serves the cement, pulp and paper, and ferro-alloy material industries.
