Capital Bars Plc is engaged in the operation of bars, hotels and restaurants. The company was formerly known as Break for the Border Group Plc. It was founded in the year 1988 and has its registered office located in London, United Kingdom. The company's activities focuses on acquiring, developing and operating establishments such as bars, clubs, hotels, and restaurants, which offers a unique design, ambiance and offer a range of product, service and entertainment. It has 12 bars/clubs and three hotels in its portfolio.
