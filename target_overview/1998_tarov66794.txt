This company is engaged in the manufacture of chilled and frozen ethnic prepared meals and snacks. It was established in 1989. The company is headquartered in Egham, United Kingdom.It is a market leader in both the chilled Indian and Thai ready meal category. Its cuisines include Thai, Mexican, Indian, and Oriental. Itss ethnic dishes are Chicken Tikka Masala with Pilau Rice and Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice. Other company's ethnic meals include Kadai chicken with Spinach Pilau and Lamb do Rukha with Saffron Rice, Rogani Mushrooms, Cashew Mutter Pilau and Garlic & Coriander Naans. The company's products are retailed in Delhaize, Dunnes Stores, Sainbury's, Rewe, Budgens, Waitrose, and Morrisons. The company has worked with The Quilon Restaurant in creating products including the Malabar Banquet Box for Two, Coconut Rice, Mango and Ginger Chicken, Chicken Coconut Masala, Potato and Cauliflower Stir Fry, and Two Malabar Parathas. The company has also worked with The Bombay Brasserie with products including Hyderabadi Bhuna Chicken, Chicken Tikka Makhani, Khatte Meethe Aloo, Mushroom Pilau, Spinach Pilau, Tandoori Naan, Lamb Jardaloo, Bengali Prawn Curry, Chicken Dakshini and Chicken Kolhapuri.The company's manufacturing facilities are in Windmill Lane, Collett Way, and Dean Way.
