This company is engaged in the manufacture of chocolate and cocoa products. It was incorporated in 1926. The registered business office of the company is located in Johannesburg, South Africa. The company is considered as a leading South African food group, and a market leader in the chocolate confectionery and concentrated beverage markets. It offers a wide range of products such as chocolate and sugar confectionary, cocoa and beverages; concentrates for the carbonated soft drink industry; squashes, cordials, bases, sport drinks and in-home carbonating syrups; jellies, packaged desserts, baking aids, glace, cherries, jams and marmalades. The company's products are marketed under a number of well known brands including Cadbury, Chappies, Oros and Energade. It operates as a subsidiary of Cadbury plc.
