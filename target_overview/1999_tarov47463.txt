This company is engaged in the provision of construction and engineering services in South Korea and internationally. It is involved in the public, business airport, sale, logistics, education, research culture, medical correction, and military facilities construction activities; airport road, railway housing, and harbour overseas construction activities; as well as the undertaking of apartment residential complexes and environment/plant facilities. The company, established in 1967, was formerly known as Kumho Tyre Co. Ltd. The company has its registered head office in Naju-si, Jeollanam-do, Republic of Korea. It is registered as a limited company. The company's construction business unit mainly undertakes the activity of building apartment complexes and expands its construction work to overseas markets, including Vietnam. The company is active in applying physical laws and principles of engineering in the design, development, and utilisation of machines, materials, instruments, structures, processes, and systems.
