This company is engaged in the manufacture of aircraft oxygen and life support systems, aircraft fuel systems, and flight-deck controls. The company, along with ECE, IN-LHC, Precilec and IN-FLEX constitutes the Aircraft Systems division of Groupe Zodiac, a French aerospace and nautical equipment maker. The company was incorporated in 1963. The company has developed unprecedented experience in fuel circulation systems for civil and military aircraft. Its activity are in the fields: fuel circulation (circulation, fuel management), systems monitoring and management, on-board electronics monitoring and measurement of temperature and humidity, optronics and image processing, and oxygen and life support. The company also produces industrial flowmeters and telecommunications equipment, such as remote transmissions systems and computer-aided telephony. The company also offers refueling systems, ground refueling systems, and in-flight refueling systems for aircraft and helicopters. It carries out research and development, qualification, integration and certification support regarding fuel circulation systems for aircraft, helicopters and missiles. It is the exclusive supplier of refueling systems for Formula 1 racing. It has set up a repair and maintenance center to ensure ultra-fast and effective service to its top priority end customers. A world leader in the manufacture of flight oxygen masks, the company as of 2006 has contracts that include: fuel system for the Russian Regional Jet, fuel circulation and fuel quantity system for the latest Dassault Falcon F7X business jet, fuel circulation system for the A380. Besides operations in France, the company has subsidiaries in China, Germany, Indonesia, the United Kingdom, and the United States of America.
