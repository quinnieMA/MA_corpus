The company is engaged in providing management holding services for its diverse range of local business units in the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in 1961 and has its registered head office located in London, United Kingdom. The company holds shares of its local business units and regards itself as a service provider for the group. The business policy is governed by the members of the holding company plus members of the advisory board who are elected by its local business units. Among other things, the holding company renders services in the area of managerial finance, business support services, corporate and transaction structure and advice, management information system, strategic management and legal advice, and other investment advisory financial services.
