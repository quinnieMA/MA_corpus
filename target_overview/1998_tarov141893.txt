The firm, formerly known as Kent International Airport Plc, is a privately held company which is engaged in the provision of scheduled air transportation services. It was incorporated in January of 1980. The registered head office of the company is strategically located in London, United Kingdom. The company is primarily involved in providing air transportation of passengers or freight over regular routes and on regular schedules. It operates under the management and supervision of its executive director, Mr. Marti Keith May. The company is affiliated with KPMG Audit Plc, which serves as its business auditor. Its business operation is principally active in the United States. The company serves as one of the wholly owned subsidiaries of Planestation Group Plc, which is engaged in property development.
