Harpur Group Limited, with registered head office located in Swindon, United Kingdom, is a holding company engaged in the international processing and issuing of corporate cards. The company is also involved in the sale of out-sourced back office maintenance and fuel processing to the automotive market. Its subsidiary, Harpur UK, is a fuelcard specialist that runs Dialcard Limited and Overdrive Credit Card Limited. The group's customers are drivers and fleet managers. Harpur Group Limited, formerly known as Vetnet Limited, is a private company which was incorporated in May 1989.
