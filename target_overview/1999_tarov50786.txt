This company is engaged in the manufacture of wearing apparel and accessories. It specialises in the manufacture of lingerie such as tights and underwear. Its product offering includes casual tights and corsetry, stockings, brassiere, panties, girdles, half slip, camisole, pantyhose, among others. The company also offers a range of accessories such as handkerchief and gloves. The company was incorporated in 1926. With registered business address located in Fresnoy le Grand, France, it also has operation in the United Kingdom, Belgium, France, and Italy. The company operates is a majority owned subsidiary of CSP International Fashion Group S.p.A., an Italy-based company which is engaged in the manufacture of underwear under the brand names Sanpellegrino, Le Bourget, Oroblu, and Lepel.
