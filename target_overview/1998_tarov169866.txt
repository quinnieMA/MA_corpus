The company, which is based in the United Kingdom, is engaged in the production of a range of motor vehicle supplies, accessories, tools, and equipment; and new motor vehicle parts. It was established in the year 1940 and was formerly known as Caradon Peak Limited. Its registered business address is located in Tipton.Some of the company's products are spare automobile parts such as belts, brakes, air filter, alternator, clutch, connecting rod, crankshaft, exhaust pipe, engine testing equipment, exhaust valve, fuel injector, piston ring, rack and pinion, automotive hoists, windscreen wiper, windshield, starter ring gear, steering wheel, rear-view mirror, and rocker arm. Its customers include automobile manufacturers.
