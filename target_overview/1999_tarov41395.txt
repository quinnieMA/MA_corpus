This company, with registered business office located in London, United Kingdom, is a holding firm for a group engaged in the provision of digital imaging services and specialist printing. It provides group planning and strategy and in particular exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top managements, and provides central services. The company is part of Schawk Inc, the world's largest and most experienced brand imaging solutions provider. The company specializes in two markets such as packaging and advertising and has three core competency groups like graphic services & prepress, brand consulting & design and enterprise products. It creates, develops, implements, and measures comprehensive knowledge-based imaging solutions that enable brand-driven organizations to drive maximum value through each of their brands globally. It helps companies unleash the power that brands have to grow and become part of people's lives everywhere.
