This company, with registered office in Rochdale, United Kingdom, is engaged in the wholesale distribution of security and low voltage products. It was incorporated in 1986. The company specialises in the wholesale distribution of electronic security and surveillance systems and related products. It offers a range of products such as intrusion, CCTV/video, access control, fire, sound & communication, integrated IP solutions and other related products. It also provides services including field & telephone technical support, training, on-site installation support and service & repair. The company has established itself as a leading trade only supplier of security system equipment with branches through out the United Kingdom. It operates more than 20 distribution stores in the country.
