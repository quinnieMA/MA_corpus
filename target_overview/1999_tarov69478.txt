The company is engaged in the manufacture of insulation materials and hydrocarbon resins as well as provision of water transport services. The company has a registered business office based in Uithoorn, the Netherlands.The company is recognized as an international leader in the tar and asphalt industry specializing in the refinery and processing of crude coal tar. The company offers portfolio of products, such as: Naphtalene, an intermediate for a whole palette of dyes in the clothing industry and other applications in synthetic lubricants, concrete preparation and in the paper industry; Cindu Creosote oil, a wood preserving product for industrial usage in wood impregnating plant; Carbon Black, specifically used in the rubber industry as a blackening agent; Cindu Tar Compounds, which are used in the paint and coating industries.The company also caters to the needs in the steel and aluminum industries.
