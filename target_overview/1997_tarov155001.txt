The company is a public limited firm primarily engaged in the development and selling of real estate. The company, formerly known as Trushelfco (No. 491) Limited, was incorporated in August of 1982. The registered head office of the company is located in Manchester, United Kingdom.The company acts as agents or brokers in selling, buying and renting real estate for others. The company is also involved in the renovation and re-lease of existing buildings and the sale of improve parcels to others. Moreover, the company purchases a tract of land; determines the marketing of the property; develops the building program and design; obtains the necessary public approvals and financing; builds the structure; and leases, manages and sells the property.
