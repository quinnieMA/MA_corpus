The company is engaged in the distribution of all types of electronic equipment primarily in Belgium. Based in Brussels, Belgium, it was acquired by Spector Photo Group Sa in July 2002.In addition, the company offers a wide range of products, such as televisions, videos, computers, computer games, telephones, and cameras. It also provides hi-fi and software applications. Through its subsidiary, Hifi International SA, the company is active in Luxembourg. Hifi International SA is engaged in the retail sale of electrical household appliances.
