This company, with registered office address in Dunstable, United Kingdom, is engaged in the ownership and operation of hotels and retail pubs and restaurants. The company, formerly known as Swallow Group Limited which lasted until January 2003, was incorporated in 1927. As of March 2005, the company disposed off its subsidiaries containing 46 of its Marriott hotels to a fellow company within the Whitbread group. At the same time, the company acquired Premier Inns Limited including its wholly owned subsidiary, Premier Travel Inn Limited. The company is a part of Whitbread PLC, the United Kingdom's leading hospitality company that manages several popular brands of hotels, restaurants and health and fitness clubs, including Premier Travel Inn, Brewers Fayre, Beefeater, Costa and David Lloyd Leisure. Whitebread was originally founded as a brewery by Samuel Whitbread and Thomas Shewell in 1742 but has since halted all interests in brewing.
