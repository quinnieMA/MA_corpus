The company, formerly known as Roachbeck Limited, is a privately-held entity engaged in the wholesale distribution of motor vehicles. It was incorporated in 1988 and conducts its business from its corporate head office located in Crawley, United Kingdom. It is primarily involved in the provision of a selection of vehicles. It also offers other services, such as anti-rust treatment of motor vehicles , auto electrical repairs and service , bodywork repair of motor vehicles , car body repair , commercial vehicle servicing and repairs ,motor vehicle gas conversion , and many others offered to a variety of customers in the region.
