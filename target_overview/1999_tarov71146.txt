This company is engaged in the manufacture of hand and edge tools, and office equipments. The registered business address of the company is located in Strasbourg (Bas-Rhin), France. The company was formed in 1989 through the merger of Strafor and Facom. The company is one of the world's leading manufacturers of hand tools, equipment, and parts for the garage market and automotive aftermarket. It covers specialised tools for the automotive, aerospace, electrical engineering, electronics, offshore, and heavy duty industry markets, as well as tools for the maintenance, general applications, and consumer markets. The company operates 13 production facilities in Europe and the United States, while sales and support services are provided through 15 centres located in Europe, the United States, and Asia, which provide distribution to more than 100 countries through a network of 5,500 distributors.
