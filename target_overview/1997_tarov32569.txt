This company is engaged in engineering and technological services. It was incorporated in 1932 and has its registered business office based in Alcobendas, Spain. The company specialises in crude oil, natural gas, refined fuels, and renewable energy solutions, alongside providing engineering and technological services to support global energy needs. It includes activities related to the exploration, extraction, and production of oil and natural gas resources worldwide. It aims to be a leading global energy company, providing reliable and sustainable energy solutions that meet the evolving needs of society.
