The entity is a holding company of the Guilbert Group with interests in the trade, purchase and selling of stationery products. The Guilbert Group is a leading European contract stationer. The company was incorporated in July 1959. The registered business address of the company is located in Senlis, France. As a holding company, it has been taking part in financing and conducting direct concern in its operating businesses as well as in its interests in other ventures. The company holds and manages assets of more than 20 companies that are owned and controlled by Guilbert and Office Depot, Inc (its parent company). These companies include Guilbert France, which is engaged in the trade of stationery and office supplies; and Office Depot UK Limited (formerly Guilbert UK Limited), which is engaged in the distribution of stationary and supplies, computer supplies, printed materials and office furniture. Besides France and the United Kingdom, the company's operation extends to Germany, Spain, the Netherlands, Italy, Portugal, Ireland, Luxembourg and Belgium. The company is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Office Depot International BV, a mail order company active in the field of office supplies, office furniture, computer utilities and equipment.
