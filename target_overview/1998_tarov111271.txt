The privately-held company is engaged the design, manufacture and supply of precision machine tools for a wide range of applications. It was founded by Frank Shipman and Frederick Pollard in 1899. Its head office is located in Leicester, United Kingdom.The company is a world leader in the supply of precision surface, cylindrical and creepfeed grinding machines and systems. It supplies ultra precision components to diverse industries, including aerospace; automotive; toolroom; power generation; mould, tool and die; and medical industries. Its portfolio consists of the following: cylindrical, surface, and creepfeed grinders; bone reamers, knee and hip joints, needles, cannulas and bone saws for medical instruments and components; intelligent machining solutions; thin-wall transmission components for F1 racing team suppliers, and hub components for automotive first tier suppliers; and aerospace components, including firtrees, nozzle guide vanes, shroud segments, honeycombs, and airfoils.The company is a subsidiary of Kellenberger, a global designer, manufacturer and marketer if high-precision manual and CNC (computer numerical controlled) straight, angular, and universal cylindrical grinders.
