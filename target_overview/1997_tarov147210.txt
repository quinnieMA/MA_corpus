The firm is a privately held company which is engaged in the operation and management of a department store. It was incorporated in October of 1971. The registered head office of the company is strategically located in Glasgow, United Kingdom. The company sells various merchandise lines, such as apparel (shirts, dresses, skirts, and trousers); jewelry (bracelets, necklace, earrings, and rings), home furnishings (home appliances); linens; books; and footwear (shoes, boots, and sneakers), each with separate cash registers and sales associates. Its business operation is principally active in the United Kingdom. The company is affiliated with PricewaterhouseCoopers Llp, which serves as its business auditor. It operates as one of the subsidiaries of Algagrove Limited, which is engaged in the management activities of a holding company.
