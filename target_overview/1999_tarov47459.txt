The company, formerly known as Servomex PLC, is a British-based firm that operates as a holding company for a group of subsidiaries engaged in the design and manufacture of industrial instrumentation and systems. The company was incorporated in August 1985 and has its registered office in Crowborough, the United Kingdom.One of the company's subsidiaries, Servomex Group Limited, is recognized as a leading supplier of high performance reliable gas analysis solutions. The company and its subsidiaries principally conduct business operations within the United Kingdom.
