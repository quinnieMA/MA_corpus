The company, formerly known as Plasmec Limited, is engaged in the manufacture of electrical equipment. It was incorporated in July of 1963. The registered head office of the company is strategically located St. Austell, United Kingdom. The company offers a range of products which include precious metal contact springs, precision components of metal and plastic, plastic moulding tools, mechanical assemblies, plugs and sockets for communications and electronic systems. These products are primarily distributed in the domestic market. It is affiliated with PricewaterhouseCoopers Llp, which serves as its business auditor. The company is a wholly owned subsidiary of West Pharmaceutical Services Group Limited, an English company involved in the manufacture of rubber and plastic components, and the construction of tooling for rubber and plastic components used in the healthcare industry.
