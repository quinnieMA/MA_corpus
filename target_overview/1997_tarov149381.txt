The company is engaged in the retail distribution of motor vehicles as well as its parts and accessories. It was incorporated in July of 1971 and has its registered head office in the city of Birmingham in the United Kingdom.The company's automobiles are new and used cars, trucks, crossovers and SUVs (sport utility vehicles) under different brands. It further markets such automobile parts and accessories as radiators and shock absorbers as well as wheels. As part of its business, the company offers car leasing, financing and insurance services as well as repair and maintenance services. The company markets its products throughout Birmingham as well as the surrounding areas.
