This company is engaged in the manufacture and distribution of steel products and recycled metal. The company, formerly known as Co-Steel Sheerness Plc, was founded in 1966. The registered head office of the company is located in Cardiff, United Kingdom. The company specialises in the manufacture of pig iron in molten or solid form. The company is also involved in making steel and manufacturing shapes, such as bar, plate, rod, sheet, strip, and wire. Its mission is to lead the steel industry by providing quality, sustainable steel products while promoting responsible recycling practices to minimise environmental impact.
