This company, formerly known as Specialeyes PLC, is engaged in the ownership and operation of optical stores in the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in December 1984 and has its head office located in Leeds. The company sells a wide selection of eyeglasses, contact lenses, frames and sunglasses from the top fashion designers, including Prada, Gucci, Police, Armani, Dolce & Gabanna and Oakley. It also offers free consultation, advice and treatment for solutions to a range of eye conditions, such as implantable contact lens (ICL), phakic intraocular lens (phakic IOL), cataract surgery and refractive lens exchange (RLE). The company is a subsidiary of DCM Optical Holdings Ltd., a UK-based provider of optical healthcare services and supplies. It is the UK's leading provider of laser eye surgery and private lens surgery, offering a range of eye care services including laser eye surgery, lens replacement, cataract surgery, and general eye health services.
