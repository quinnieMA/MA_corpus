The company, formerly known as Marlow Group Holdings Public Limited Company, is engaged in the management activities of a holding company. It is a private limited company which was incorporated in January of 1990. The registered head office of the company is located in Westerham, United Kingdom.The company provides group planning and strategy and in particular exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top managements, and provides central services. It also holds the securities and other equity interests of companies and enterprises for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms. It operates under the administration and supervision of David Joseph Wilcox.
