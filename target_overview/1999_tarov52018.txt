Themis FTSE Fledgling Index Trust PLC is engaged as an investment trust company. It was incorporated in 1994 and was previously operating under the name The Hoare Govett 1000 Index Investment Trust PLC. The company has a registered business office located in London in the United Kingdom.Themis FTSE Fledgling Index Trust invests primarily in companies operating in the United Kingdom. Some of the company's major investments are in firms such as Ockham PLC, Zergo Holdings PLC, French Connection Group PLC, Vega Group PLC and Television Corporation PLC. In addition, it is affiliated with Ernst & Young firm which acts as its auditing body.
