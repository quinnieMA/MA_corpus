MTL Trust Holdings Limited is a private holding company based in the United Kingdom whose subsidiaries are engaged in the operation of buses, coaches and trains. It was incorporated in the year of 1992 and has its registered business address located in the city of Sunderland.The company is involved in holding the securities of its subsidiaries for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms. It is also responsible for performing various corporate services including financing, investment, legal affairs, tax affairs and other related services.MTL Trust Holdings operates as a subsidiary of Arriva Plc, one of the largest transport services organizations in Europe with operations in Czech Republic, Denmark, Italy, Germany, the Netherlands, Portugal, Spain and Sweden.
