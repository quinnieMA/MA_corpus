This company, with registered office address in Northampton, the United Kingdom, is engaged in providing enforcement and debt recovery services to local authorities and commercial landlords across Britain. It was incorporated in 1996 and was formerly known as Equitable Revenue Holdings Limited. The company provides individual service package for every client, based on their specific needs and requirements, from local taxation and parking enforcement to sundry debt collection and high court enforcement. It offers transferring CCJs up the high court through its free transfer-up service; recovering specific items of property; evicting squatters, trespassers and protesters from private land and property; recovering rent arrears and handling repossessions for residential and commercial landlords; and tracing tenants who've left in breach of their agreement, or with rent unpaid. It serves clients across the UK includes central government departments, London Boroughs, Metropolitans and District Councils, and some of Britain's biggest private-sector companies.
