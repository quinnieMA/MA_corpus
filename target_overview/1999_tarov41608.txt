Baan Company NV is a provider of open systems and client/server based Enterprise Resource Planning software, based upon revenue data provided by industry market research firms. The company was founded in 1983 as Baan Automatisering BV. Its registered office is located in Barneveld, Netherlands.Baan Company is a prominent deliverer of software in the field of running a company for open systems in client/server organization. The company's products hold the front-office functions (such as the interaction between clients and sale) to back office functions (such as the purchase and storage management). Open system enables businesses to respond to the demands of competitive market place. The company's ERP system permits organizations to manage resources across the enterprise and enables the integration of sales management, component procurement, inventory management, manufacturing control, project management, distribution, transportation, finance and other functions.Baan Company's products are sold by the two heading branch offices in the Netherlands and the USA, by the international service centers in the Netherlands, the USA and India, as well as via direct and indirect distribution channels in a wide range of countries. The company's line of software products is sold under the name Triton. The Triton family of computer software products consists of three product lines: Triton Applications, Triton OrgWare, and Triton Tools. The company's Triton family of software products has been designed for rapid implementation, flexibility and scaleability.
