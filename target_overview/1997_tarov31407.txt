The company is engaged in providing music publishing and sound recording studio services in London. The company was incorporated in May of 1980 and has its registered head office located in London, United Kingdom. The company provides technical expertise for sound recording through its operation of recording facilities and ownership of a portfolio of song copyrights. It acquires, protects, administers, exploits, and licenses musical compositions. The products of the company are musical recordings, lyrics, and musical scores, master recordings, audio compact discs, cassettes, laser discs, and databases to distribute downloads of recordings.
