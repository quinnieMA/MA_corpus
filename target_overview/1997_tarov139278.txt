The company is engaged in the wholesale distribution of sugar and confectionery products. It was incorporated in October of 1979 and conducts its business from its registered head office located in Northampton, United Kingdom.The company primarily sells the following products: raw sugar, liquid sugar, refined sugar from sugarcane, raw cane sugar, sugarbeets, boiled sweets, toffees, marshmallows, fondant, lollipops, candy bars, gum drops, and licorice candy. In addition, the company sells other sweet products, such as jelly beans, gummies, fudge, chocolates, nougat, lemon drops, peppermint drops, and other sweet items of snack food. Further, the company delivers products at competitive prices in accordance with customers' overall needs and the volume required in each particular case.
