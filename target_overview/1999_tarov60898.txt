The company, formerly known as Anagen PLC, is engaged in the provision of general construction services. It was incorporated in February 1993. The registered head office of the company is located in London, United Kingdom.The company specializes in the construction of a wide variety of buildings, such as industrial plants, museums, shopping centers, high-rise office buildings, medical centers, sports facilities, single family detached houses, town houses, and row houses.The company's capabilities are demolition; clean up; foundations; masonry; post tension cable; steel erection; electrical; plumbing; heating & cooling; steel & wood framing; insulation; drywall; painting; finish carpentry; flooring; complete kitchen & bathroom remodeling; second story additions; room additions; building expansions; concrete work; porches & decks; new replacement windows; new replacement doors; roofing; siding; and gutters.
