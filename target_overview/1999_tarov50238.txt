This company, formerly known as AB Gas-accumulator and AB Svenska Gasaccumulator, is a gas company that is engaged in the production of industrial and medical gases. The company was founded in 1904 and has a registered business address located in Lidingo, Sweden. Its main products include oxygen, nitrogen and argon. Other products are: fuel gases acetylene and propane, as well as hydrogen, carbon dioxide and nitrous oxide. The company is known as one of the world's largest gas companies, which manufactures and markets gases and services to industrial, medical and specialty gas markets in more than 40 countries in Europe, Latin America and the United States of America. Aside from its primary activities, it is also involved in the sale of welding equipments. Its products are marketed under the trademarks: Mison, Accura, Cirrus, HiQ, Cumulus, Biopuster, Atmofry, Lasser Line, Mapax, Rappid Processing and Toolvac. The company is a cooperating partner of SMTE, a specialist in making designs for the steel and mining industries.
