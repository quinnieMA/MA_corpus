This company, formerly known as UK Paper PLC, primarily operates as a holding firm of a group involved in the manufacture of paper products. It was incorporated in April 1986 and has its registered head office located in London, United Kingdom. The company is focused exclusively on the acquisition and strategic management of its operating units. It is active in providing management and support services for the entire group in administrative affairs, financial matters and public relations. The company predominantly operates in the United Kingdom. It is a subsidiary of the Fletcher Challenge Industries UK Limited, which is involved in forestry, pulp and paper activities.
