This company is an entity based in France that is primarily engaged in letting of properties. It started its operations in September 1990 and has its registered business office strategically located in Paray Vieille Poste, France.The company is principally involved in renting or leasing real estate to others; managing real estate for others; selling, buying, or renting real estate for others; and providing other real estate related services, such as appraisal services. Moreover, the company acts as lessor of buildings used as residences or dwellings, such as single-family homes, apartment buildings, and town homes. It serves residential, commercial and industrial clients. The company has its active operations within the country.
