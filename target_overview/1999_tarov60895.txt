The company, based in Germany, is engaged in the retail sale and provision of telecommunication products and solutions. It was founded in 1991. The company has a registered business headquarters located in Stuttgart in Germany.Since its establishment, the company has grown to be recognized as one of the largest network-independent European telecom service and mobile telecommunications service providers. It serves wireless, wireline, and Internet customers in Europe. The company occupies a premier position in the European telecommunications market. It is able to provide telephone communications, radiotelephone communications, and other related communications services. The company offers mobile and cellular phones, software applications, Information Technology (IT) solutions, ring tones, personal computers, notebooks, laptops, and related telecommunication solutions and services.
