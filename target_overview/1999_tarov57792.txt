The company, formerly known as Winglaw Group Limited, primarily operates as a holding firm whose subsidiaries are active in the real estate industry. It is a private limited firm, which was incorporated in August 1957 and has its registered head office located in London, United Kingdom.The company provides management and support services for its subsidiaries in administrative affairs, financial matters and public relations. The company, through its wholly owned subsidiary Park Street Properties Ltd., is involved in property investment and trading, property management and construction. The holding company is globally owned by Warner Estate Holdings, a real estate investment trust (REIT) involved in the property investment and asset management activities.
