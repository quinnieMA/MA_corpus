This company is engaged in manufacturing and supply of manmade fiber machinery or textile machinery. The company has registered office address located in Remscheid-Lennep, Germany. It was founded in 1922. Barnag is active in the area of spinning systems for polyester, nylon and polypropylene and texturing machines. The company's core competencies include the manufacture of components such as winders, pumps and godets for many applications at the Remscheid site. It also offers a comprehensive after-sales service. In 2005, Barmag sold its heat treatment assets to Bodycote International PLC, the world's leading supplier of metallurgical services to industry. Ownership of these assets will allow Bodycote to provide Barmag with a cost effective solution to its heat treatment requirements which are primarily located in the automotive, tooling and textile industries in Germany. The company is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Saurer Group of Switzerland, the worldwide leading manufacturer of chemical fiber or textile machines.
