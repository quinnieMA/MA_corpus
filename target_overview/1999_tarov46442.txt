The company is a Spanish-based, privately-held entity, which is engaged in furnishing shipping information and arranging transportation for freight and cargo. It was incorporated in November of 1995 and has registered head office located in Santander, Spain.The company specializes in hauling cargo for air and ocean freight forwarders and airlines, coordinates entire process from choosing the right carrier and containers to reliable shipment. Its activities also include warehousing, storage and distribution. In addition, the company offers supplier management, pre-sorting, picking, assembling and product packaging, barcode labeling, quality and quantity control, transport insurance and customs clearance.
