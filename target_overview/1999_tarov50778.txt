This company is engaged in the production and trade of a diverse range of devices from single transistors to microprocessors. It was incorporated in 1987, and was previously named as SGS-Thomson Microelectronics N.V. The company has a registered head office based in Schiphol, Netherlands. The company offers products include semiconductor integrated circuits and discrete devices, microcontrollers, Smartcard products, standard commodity components, micro-electro-mechanical systems and advanced analog products, application-specific integrated circuits, as well as application-specific standard products for analog, digital, and mixed-signal applications. The company also offers subsystems and modules for the telecommunications, automotive, and industrial markets comprising mobile phone accessories, battery chargers, ISDN power supplies, and in-vehicle equipment for electronic toll payment. The company's products are used in various microelectronic applications consisting of automotive products, computer peripherals, telecommunications systems, consumer products, industrial automation, and control systems.
