The company is a private entity primarily engaged in the operation and management of sports facilities and leisure establishments in the U.K. It operates racetracks, skiing facilities, soccer fields, bowling centers, marinas, as well as golf courses and country clubs. In addition, the company also functions as a holding entity and is involved in the provision of administrative and management services to its subsidiaries and affiliates. Formerly known as Gamehost Limited, the company was incorporated in 1985 and is based in London. Moreover, it operates as a subsidiary of PlaneStation PLC, an operator of airports in Europe and North America. PlaneStation holds a major stake in EUJet, an Irish low-fare carrier.
