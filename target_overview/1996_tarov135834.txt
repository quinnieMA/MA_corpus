The company, based in the United Kingdom, is engaged in the design and production of a range of solutions for floorcoverings, barrier matting, wall protection, soft furnishing and lighting design, in addition to a range of stair edging and floor trim profiles. It was established in 1966 and was formerly known as Gradus Limited. Its registered corporate headquarters is located in Altrincham.Some of the company's products are stair edgings, step & aisle lighting, stair trims & welded corners, stair rods, safety track & titazel, transition strips, carpet tile, broadloom and impervious backed carpets, barrier matting systems. timber handrail, stainless steel wall protection system, dual rail, wall guards & wall strips, bed protectors & locator, corner guards, movement joints, drainage systems and soft furnishings. Its main customers are the commercial, retail, leisure, healthcare, hospitality and education sectors.
