This company is engaged in the provision of waste management services. Its services include recycling, composting, household waste recycling site management, transfer stations, power generation, energy from waste incineration, clinical and confidential waste services, hazardous waste treatment, commercial liquid and dry waste collection, site restoration and remediation of contaminated land, safe and efficient landfill disposal, and waste auditing and minimisation services. The companys operation complies with the highest professional and environmental standards. It serves both public and private sector customers. It was incorporated in December 1978, the company was formerly known as Terry Adams Limited. The company conducts its business in its registered office strategically located in Exeter, United Kingdom.
