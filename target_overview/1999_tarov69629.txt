The company, based in the United Kingdom, operates as a holding company whose subsidiaries are engaged in the operation of charter air services throughout Europe; and distribution of fresh produce, temperature-controlled and ambient products to supermarkets and wholesale markets throughout the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in the year 1997 and was formerly known as 2049th Single Member Shelf Investment Company Limited. Its registered business address is located in Leeds.The company is involved in holding the securities of its subsidiaries for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms. In addition, it is also responsible in the provision of a range of corporate services for its subsidiaries including the areas of financing, investment, legal affairs, tax affairs and other related services.
