The company, formerly The Harbour Club Limited, is engaged in the management and operation of fitness and well-being center in the United Kingdom. It was incorporated in April 1991 and has its registered head office located in New Malden, Surrey. The company's fitness and well-being center provides a comprehensive range of health services comprising on-site health assessments, health advices through it professionally-trained advisors, relaxation areas, health education seminars and physiotherapy to help its customers look after their health on every level. Its objective is to improve its customers' life for the long-term by offering an innovative combination of fitness facilities, health services and expert staff.The company is ultimately-owned by Nuffield Health, a not-for-profit organization that operates a network of 30 private hospitals and 52 fitness and well-being centers throughout the United Kingdom.
