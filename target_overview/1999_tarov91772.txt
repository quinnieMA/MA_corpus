This company is engaged in the manufacture of industrial inorganic chemicals and allied products. It started its operations in 1917. The registered business office of the company is strategically located in Seadrift, United States of America. The company offers a wide range of products that include industrial cleaners, paint booth chemistry products, pre-treatments, food and dairy products, metalworking lubricants and coolants, specialty chemicals, pulp and paper chemicals, plastics, janitorial products, water treatment chemicals and other related products. It also offers chemical formulating, custom processing and toll blending, packaging and private labeling services.
