Construcciones Lain SA is a Spanish-based company primarily general construction of buildings and civil engineering works. Construcciones Lain SA was established in 1963 as a subsidiary of the British John Laing Construction group which focuses on the public sector infrastructure assets in the United Kingdom. In 1999, Construcciones Lain SA merged with Obrascon Huarte and formed Obrascon Huarte Lain SA. From then on, the newly-formed company's business lines are construction (includes the construction of highways, bridges, tunnels, and residential properties), Infrastructure Concessions (relate to transport, car parks and ports) and Services. The company's customers include: the Spanish Ministry for civil works, universities, regional councils and general contractors.
