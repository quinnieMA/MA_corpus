Cardinal Health UK 100 Limited, with registered head office located in Romford, United Kingdom, is engaged in providing a wide variety of products and services throughout the United Kingdom. The company offers IV delivery systems, temperature management and disposables under the Alaris brand; generic drugs and other specialist pharmaceuticals under the Martindale name; distribution, gowns, surgical instruments; and automated dispensing systems and medication and supply use processes under the Pyxis brand. Its customers include hospitals and other healthcare providers, retail pharmacies, and suppliers. Cardinal Health UK 100 Limited, formerly known as IPD Group Limited, is a private company which was incorporated in December 1989. The company is a subsidiary of the United States-based Cardinal Health Inc., the leading provider of products and services supporting the health care industry.
