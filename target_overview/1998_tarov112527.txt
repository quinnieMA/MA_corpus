The company is primarily engaged in real estate business, specialising in the development, investment, and management of properties. It was incorporated in 1697, and has its registered head office located in Belfast, United Kingdom.The company leases, develops, and invests in various properties, which are purely for industrial, commercial, and residential purposes. Its scope of business involves bringing together owners of land and people who are looking for property on which to conduct a certain business activity. The company provides expertise about locating, appraising, and conveying legal rights to such properties.The company predominantly operates in the United Kingdom. It serves a wide range of residential and commercial clients all over the country. In 1998, the company was acquired by Dunloe House Plc, and was renamed to Dunloe Ewart.
