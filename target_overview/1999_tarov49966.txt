The company is primarily engaged in water supply and irrigation systems. It is involved in the operation of water treatment plants and/or the operation of water supply systems. The water supply system may include pumping stations, aqueducts, and/or distribution mains. The water may be used for drinking, irrigation and other uses. The US-based company has a registered office located in Harrington Park, New Jersey.The company was founded in 1869 as the Hackensack Water Company. It is a leader in the water services industry since its foundation. The company is the operator of 226 municipal water systems, including three of the nation's largest contract services operations.It is a member of a global family of water companies that are dedicated to sharing knowledge and delivering world-class service in the communities they serve. The company also is a leader in numerous industry-based organizations that promote best practices for water treatment: American Water Works Association, National Association of Water Companies, Water Environment Federation, and National Association of Clean Water Agencies.Across the country, the company serves both as an owner-operator of regulated utilities and as an operator of municipal and industrial systems through public-private partnerships and contract services.
