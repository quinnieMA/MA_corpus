The company, based in France, operates as a waste management company involved in the provision of various waste services, including waste collection, which includes domestic, hazardous and non-hazardous industrial wastes; and sorting, recycling and biological recovery; incineration with waste-to-energy recovery. Its registered head office is located in Nanterre Cedex.The company also provides landfill disposal services for household and industrial wastes, urban cleansing sanitation and industrial maintenance, and the rehabilitation of polluted industrial sites. In 2005, the company and ROS ROCA, a company of Spanish origin and leader on the waste management equipment and technologies market, have signed a partnership agreement to develop pneumatic domestic waste collection on the French market. The system permits waste to be collected without trucks circulating in cities, via pneumatic equipment. Particularly adapted to the development of new urban zones and renovation works in historical city centers, pneumatic collection differs from traditional collection in that it eliminates the placing of waste on public highways, truck circulation and all of the nuisances related to this activity.
