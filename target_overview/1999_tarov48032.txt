The company, with registered office located in Hatfield, United Kingdom, is engaged in the production of high quality lines of die-cast replica items. The company's products are sold through retail channels in the United Kingdom, and in the United States. The company has produced British TV for model ideas, such as Mr. Bean's Mini, Inspector Morse's Jaguar and even models based on Enid Blyton's Noddy tales. A variety of firms ranging from Cadbury's chocolate, Guinness beer to Eddie Stobart haulage let the company scale models made out of their road vehicles. In 1995, the company introduced a new range of 1/76th scale UK and Hong Kong bus models under the Original Omnibus Company banner. The company was established in 1995. It has its origins in the Corgi Toy brand introduced by the toy car company Mettoy Playcraft Ltd. in 1956.The company is the leading name in die-cast scale models and one of the United Kingdom's best known brands. Its famous model vehicles captured the imagination of millions of baby boomers and, what were once simply toys for boys, are highly sought-after in the expanding collectibles market.
