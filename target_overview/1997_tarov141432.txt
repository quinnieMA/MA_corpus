The company is a privately held entity based in the United Kingdom that is engaged in the manufacture and distribution of ceramic wall and floor tiles. It started its operations in January of 1996. The registered business office of the company is located in Manchester, UK. The company is principally involved in manufacturing and dealing ceramics, earthware, China fire and all allied bricks, tiles, tezaro, and other related products. These are primarily offered to contractors, homebuilders, home development stores, and to the general public.The company has its active operations in the United Kingdom.
