The company, based in Brazil, is engaged in the provision of investment and management holding as well as related business support services. It was incorporated in the year 1994. As a holding company, it provides group planning and strategies as well as exercises financial control over its subsidiaries. It also settles the group's general policies, appoints top management, and provides central services. The company operates from a registered business headquarters located in Rio de Janeiro in Brazil.The company, through its subsidiaries and affiliate firms, is involved in the generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical power to the province of Espirito Santos.
