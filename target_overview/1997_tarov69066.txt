The company is primarily engaged in the manufacture of lightweight, coated publication-grade paper for magazines, brochures, and direct-mail advertising. The company, with headquarters located in Grand Rapids, Minnesota, was founded in the year 1901.The company produces an annual capacity of 350,000 metric tons. The company was the first North American forest products company to achieve combined ISO 14001, 9001 and OHSAS 18001 certification in the year 2002. The company operates as a subsidiary of UPM-Kymmene OYJ, a Finland-based global forest products group with core businesses in printing papers, speciality papers, label materials and wood products. This company operates in three business segments, namely Energy and Pulp, Paper, and Engineered materials.
