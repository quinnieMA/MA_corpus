The company is a provider of cellular telecommunications services in Brazil, through its subsidiary, Vivo S.A. The former provides cellular telecommunications services using global system for mobile communications (GSM)/enhanced data for GSM evolution (EDGE), code division multiple access (CDMA) and time division multiple access (TDMA) in the frequency of 850 megahertz. The company was incorporated in 1997 with registered business office in Sao Paolo, Brazil. It was formerly known as Telesp Celular Participacoes S.A.
