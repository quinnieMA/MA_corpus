The company is considered as a privately-held entity which is engaged in the operation of a variety of diaries and cheese making. It was incorporated in the year 1968 and conducts its business from its corporate head office located in Mollerusa, Spain.It is engaged in a wide array of business activities, such as manufacturing dairy products (except frozen) from raw milk and/or processed milk products; manufacturing dairy substitutes (except frozen) from soybeans and other nondairy substances; and manufacturing dry, condensed, concentrated, and evaporated dairy and dairy substitute products offered to a variety of customers, businesses and industries in the region.
