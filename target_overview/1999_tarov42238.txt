The company, with registered head office located in Milton Keynes, United Kingdom, manufactures equipment for the specialized field of the safety and control of industrial machines and processes. It trades under the brand names Guardmaster, Nelsa and Sigma Prosafe. The company offers a wide range of machinery safety devices, including all sizes of interlock switches, compact guard locking interlock switches, heavy duty guard locking interlock switches, limit switches, emergency stop switches, two hand controls. It also manufactures a range of monitoring safety relays and control units. Other products include guarding systems for machines and production processes. The product range varies from standard "off the shelf'" bolt-on guards for commonly used machines to sophisticated electronic systems such as light curtains and pressure sensitive mats. The company was incorporated in 1991.
