The company, based in the United Kingdom, is engaged in the manufacture and sale of a complete line of bathroom products under the well-known Heritage brand name for distribution throughout the United Kingdom and across the world. It was incorporated in 1994 and was formerly known as Heritage Bathrooms PLC. It conducts business from its registered head office located in London.The company primarily offers the following products: family bathroom sinks, toilets, mirrors, towel holders and rails, soap dishes, tumblers, toothbrush holders, paper holders, bathracks, toilet brushes, shaving mirrors, shower enclosures and screens, corner and freestanding baths in acrylic, water heated towel rails in wall mounted and floor-standing designs, hot and cold pillar taps, mixer taps, bath and shower mixers, shower valves, cabinets, base unit, whirlpool baths with water and air jets, and corner bath with shower cabin.
