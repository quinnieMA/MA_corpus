This company, formerly known as CIM Resources Limited, is engaged in the production and supply of coal. It is headquartered in Chatswood, Australia.The company is one of the largest pure-play coal companies globally. With its merger with Yancoal Australia, the company focuses on advanced coal mining technology including Ultra Clean Coal, Premier Char, and Longwall Top Coal Caving. The company is committed to safeguard the environment throughout all operations and continually seeks to improve production processes, waste management, and use of resources to minimize impacts. The company's key assets include Gloucester Basin (Port of Newcastle); Donaldson (Port of Newcastle), Middlemount (Bowen Basin), and Monash (Port of Newcastle).
