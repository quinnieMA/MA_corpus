Donewith No. 50 Limited is engaged in the acquisition, management and trade of investment properties in the industrial sector as well as the ownership and operation of regional port facilities in the United Kingdom. Incorporated in 1900, the company was formerly known as The Cleveland Trust Limited and was renamed as Donewith No. 50 Limited in June of 2004. The registered business address of the company is located in the city of London in the United Kingdom.Donewith No. 50 operates as a subsidiary company of Ashtenne Holdings plc, which is engaged in the management activities of its holding companies. Donewith No. 50 reported an annual operating revenue of more than $16 million.
