The company, based in the United Kingdom, operates as a holding company of a group engaged as publisher of video and music. It is a privately held company incorporated in 1988 and was formerly known as Woolworths Media Plc. Its registered business address is located in London.The company provides group planning and strategy, and in particular, exercises financial control over its subsidiaries, settles their general policy and appoints their top managements, and provides central services. It is involved in holding the securities of its subsidiaries for the purpose of owning a controlling interest and influencing the management decisions of these firms.
