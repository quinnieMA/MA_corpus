The company is a privately owned American firm engaged in the manufacture of automobile accessories. The company was incorporated in the year 1983 and conducts business from its registered head office located in Howe, Indiana.The company offers a wide range of products, including storage boxes, mud flaps / splash guards, as well as running boards, cargo management, ramps and bed protection for vans, trucks, and sport utility vehicles such as Acura, BMW, Ford, Dodge, Chrysler, Hyundai, Honda, and Isuzu. The company operates under the management and supervision of Lund International, Inc., an American company engaged in the management activities of holding companies.
