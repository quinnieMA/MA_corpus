The company, based in Luton, United Kingdom, is the leading provider of interim management solutions in the United Kingdom. It offers interim executives and project managers at senior level in various roles and business sectors in disciplines, including finance, production, supply chain management, procurement, general management, sales and marketing, information technology, and human resources. The company has currently undertaken more than 8,000 successful interim management assignments, working largely with FTSE 250 and Fortune 500 businesses. Its comprehensive due diligence process includes the verification of qualifications, reference checking and in-depth interviews designed to test a candidate's competency and personal suitability for a particular interim management role.
