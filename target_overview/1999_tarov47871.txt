The company is a US-based firm engaged in the distribution of electronic components and electromechanical products. It was originally established as the radio parts distribution arm of Columbia Radio Corporation in 1928. Its registered headquarters is located in Forth Worth, Texas. The company has approximately 300,000-plus parts available on-line and more than 135,000 products in its popular catalog. Its offerings include test and measurement equipment (analyzers, meters, probes, and oscilloscopes), cable and wire assemblies, enclosure hardware, power supplies and transformers, switches, timers, passive components, and soldering equipment. The company has approximately 56 sales branches across the United States and Canada. The company distributes a wide variety of electronic products from such companies as Agilent (a measurement company), Honeywell (a company that produces a variety of consumer products, engineering services, and aerospace systems), National Semiconductor (a semiconductor manufacturer, specializing in analog devices and subsystems), Panduit (a network and electrical solutions company), and Vishay (a manufacturer of analog switches, capacitors, diodes, inductors, integrated modules, resistors and thermistors).
