The company is engaged in the operation of music and entertainment repertoire, marketing and distribution facilities. Its business was incorporated in 1962. The company has a registered head office based in Baarn, Netherlands.The company is among the three largest producers of recorded music in the world, and the leading classical music recording firm worldwide. The company's recorded music business discovers and develops recording artists and then markets and promotes their music across a wide array of formats and platforms. The company also discovers and develops songwriters, and owns and administers copyrights to musical compositions for use in recordings, public performances, and related uses, such as films and advertisements. The company also sells artist- and music-branded products via multiple sales point such as fashion retail, live performances, and the internet. The company's activities also include the acquisition, production and marketing of repertoire; the manufacture, sale and distribution of compact discs, cassettes and records; and the production of films, television and music.
