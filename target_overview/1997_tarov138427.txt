This company, formerly known as Eland Electrical Limited, is engaged in the distribution of electrical cables and accessories. It was founded in 1975 and has its registered head office located in London, United Kingdom. The company is recognized as one of the leading international supplier of electrical cables, industrial cables, special cables, electronic cables and cable accessories. Its business operation is organized into three divisions: cable, rail, and export division. The cable division offers an expansive range of electrical cables approved to British and international standards, as well as accessories from stock and a cable sourcing service. The rail division has partnership with Network Rail, wherein its approved contractors provide a range of approved cables and accessories. The company's export division exports cables and cable accessories. The company also provides electrical cable installation for automation and process control, building and construction, communication and telecommunication, marine and defense, oil, gas and petrochemical, rail and transport Industry. It has a global presence in Africa, the Middle East , and the Americas. Its products are widely distributed in Doncaster, South Yorkshire.
